# Able Archer 83 Sourcebook - Nuclear War Scare

**Source:** National Security Archive (GWU)

Declassified primary documents on the 1983 NATO war game that nearly triggered a Soviet nuclear first strike due to Soviet misinterpretation. Includes NSC memos, CIA threat assessments, and PFIAB reports released after decades of classification. Regarded as one of the closest nuclear-war moments of the Cold War, hidden from the public until FOIA pressure.
