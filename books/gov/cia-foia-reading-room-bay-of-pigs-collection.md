# CIA FOIA Reading Room - Bay of Pigs Collection

**Source:** CIA FOIA Reading Room

Declassified CIA internal histories, cables, and post-mortems on the 1961 Bay of Pigs invasion of Cuba. Documents were withheld for decades and released under FOIA pressure. The collection reveals planning failures, chain-of-command breakdowns, and internal CIA critiques suppressed from the public record.
