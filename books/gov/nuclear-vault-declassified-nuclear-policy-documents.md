# Nuclear Vault - Declassified Nuclear Policy Documents

**Source:** National Security Archive (GWU)

A flagship National Security Archive project collecting thousands of declassified U.S. and foreign government documents on nuclear weapons policy, arms control, and nuclear close calls from the Manhattan Project to the post-Cold War era. Documents were obtained through years of FOIA and mandatory declassification review requests. Includes previously secret presidential directives and Pentagon war plans.
