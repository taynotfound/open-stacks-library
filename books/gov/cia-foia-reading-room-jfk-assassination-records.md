# CIA FOIA Reading Room - JFK Assassination Records

**Source:** CIA FOIA Reading Room

CIA records released under the President John F. Kennedy Assassination Records Collection Act of 1992. Includes intelligence cables, informant files, and internal memos previously classified for over 30 years. Some documents remain partially redacted pending ongoing declassification review.
