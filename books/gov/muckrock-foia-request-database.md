# MuckRock - FOIA Request Database

**Source:** MuckRock

A searchable, crowd-sourced database of tens of thousands of public records requests filed with federal, state, and local agencies, plus the full text of government responses and released documents. Users can browse completed FOIA requests spanning police surveillance, intelligence agencies, and immigration enforcement. Serves as a living repository of government transparency efforts.
