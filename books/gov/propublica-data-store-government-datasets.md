# ProPublica Data Store - Government Datasets

**Source:** ProPublica Data Store

ProPublica's curated collection of structured government datasets released or obtained through FOIA, covering law enforcement, immigration, health agencies, campaign finance, and federal contracting. Many datasets were compiled from legally obtained government records not otherwise publicly available in usable form. Supports data-driven accountability journalism and independent research.
