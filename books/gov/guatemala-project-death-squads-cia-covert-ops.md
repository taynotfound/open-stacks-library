# Guatemala Project - Death Squads & CIA Covert Ops

**Source:** National Security Archive (GWU)

Declassified U.S. government records on the 1954 CIA-backed coup against President Jacobo Arbenz, subsequent military regimes, and U.S. support for Guatemalan security forces implicated in genocide. Includes State Department cables, CIA operational files, and Defense Intelligence Agency reports obtained via FOIA. Documents link U.S. intelligence directly to death squad activities.
