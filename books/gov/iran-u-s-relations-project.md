# Iran-U.S. Relations Project

**Source:** National Security Archive (GWU)

Declassified U.S. government records covering the 1953 Mossadegh coup (Operation AJAX), the Iran hostage crisis, the Iran-Contra affair, and Iran nuclear negotiations. Documents span CIA, State Department, and NSC files obtained through FOIA and mandatory declassification. Provides primary-source evidence of covert U.S. interference in Iranian sovereignty.
