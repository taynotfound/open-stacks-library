# FBI Vault - Rosenberg Case Files

**Source:** FBI Vault

FBI investigative files on Julius and Ethel Rosenberg, convicted and executed in 1953 for passing atomic secrets to the Soviet Union. The released documents include surveillance reports, informant testimony, and grand jury materials. These records remain contested evidence in one of America's most debated espionage cases.
