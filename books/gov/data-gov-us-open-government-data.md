# Data.gov  -  US Open Government Data

**Source:** Data.gov  

Data.gov is the US federal government's open-data portal, hosting hundreds of thousands of datasets from agencies across all three branches. Climate, health, and environmental datasets hosted here are among those at risk of removal or quiet defunding under a hostile administration.
