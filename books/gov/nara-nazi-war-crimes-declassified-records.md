# NARA - Nazi War Crimes Declassified Records

**Source:** National Archives (NARA)

Over 8 million pages of records declassified under the Nazi War Crimes Disclosure Act of 1998, revealing U.S. government knowledge of and relationships with former Nazi officials and war criminals. Includes CIA, Army, State Department, and OSS files on Operation Paperclip scientists and SS officers recruited by U.S. intelligence. Exposes post-war U.S. decisions to shield war criminals from prosecution.
