# GovernmentAttic.org - FOIA Document Repository

**Source:** GovernmentAttic.org

An independent repository of thousands of documents released by the U.S. federal government in response to FOIA requests, spanning agency histories, inspector general reports, training manuals, and policy memoranda. Run by a private researcher since 2007, it covers obscure corners of government operations not indexed elsewhere. Free and unrestricted access to raw government disclosure records.
