# Chile Documentation Project

**Source:** National Security Archive (GWU)

Thousands of declassified State Department, CIA, and Defense Department documents on U.S. involvement in the 1973 Chilean coup, Pinochet-era human rights abuses, and Operation Condor. Many documents were released as a result of National Security Archive litigation and the Chile Declassification Project initiated under President Clinton. Provides direct evidence of U.S. knowledge of torture and assassination programs.
