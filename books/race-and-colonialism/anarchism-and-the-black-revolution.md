# Anarchism and the Black Revolution

**Source:** Sprout Distro

A pamphlet by Lorenzo Kom'boa Ervin, a Black anarchist and former political prisoner, arguing for the integration of Black liberation struggle with anarchist politics. It is one of the most important texts connecting race, colonialism, and anarchism from a lived-experience perspective. Sprout Distro offers it as a free print-ready download.
