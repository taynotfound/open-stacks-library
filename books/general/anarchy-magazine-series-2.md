# Anarchy magazine (series 2)


Complete online archive of the second series of Anarchy magazine, published by the Anarchy Collective in London in the 1970s and 1980s in 38 issues. Its editors included Charlotte Baggins, Chris Broad and Phil Ruff at different times. (Our archive of the first series of Anarchy is here.)
