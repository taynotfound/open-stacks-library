# Committee Against Repression in Italy bulletin


The newsletter of CARII, a group set up in solidarity with Italian militants attacked by the state in the 1970s and 1980s, including Antonio Negri. The group was spearheaded by Silvia Federici and George Caffentzis. The bulletin was published from 1979-1983.
