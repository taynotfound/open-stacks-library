# Committee Against Repression in Italy bulletin

The newsletter of CARII, a group set up in solidarity with Italian militants attacked by the state in the 1970s and 1980s, including Antonio Negri. The group was spearheaded by Silvia Federici and George Caffentzis. The bulletin was published from 1979-1983.

With thanks to Arlen Austin for making the scans available.

Nine issues are held by the Texas Archives of Autonomist Marxism, so we assume this is the total number published.

Help with issues missing from Libcom would be very welcome.

## CARII Bulletin #6 (1980)

Newsletter from Committee Against Repression in Italy, with an update on the prosecution of Antonio Negri.

## CARII Bulletin #7 (1981)

Newsletter of the Committee Against Repression In Italy with a summary of the cases, trial updates, commentary and campaigning.

## CARII Bulletin #8 (1983)

Newsletter of Committee Against Repression in Italy, focussing on the start of the trials of hundreds of Autonomia militants arrested four years previously in 1979. Includes a statement by the defendants, an interview with Antonio Negri, information on torture of prisoners, etc.

## CARII Bulletin #9 (1983)

Newsletter of the Committee Against Repression in Italy, including: Antonio Negri freed after being elected to Parliament, 40 new arrests, call for an amnesty for all affected prisoners, Nucleo Speciale Polizia trial, etc.
