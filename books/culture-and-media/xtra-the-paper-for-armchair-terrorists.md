# Xtra! The Paper for Armchair Terrorists


Complete online archive of Xtra! - an anarchist/autonomist newspaper published in North London from 1979 until 1982. Xtra's "Structureless Tyrants" included Simon Read (who infamously infiltrated the National Front's HQ Excalibur House in Shoreditch and became a key witness in the inquiry set up to investigate it). Martin Wright (who would later be involved with Class War) was also a contributor.
