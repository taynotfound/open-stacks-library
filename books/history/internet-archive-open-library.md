# Internet Archive Open Library

**Source:** Open Library / Internet Archive

Open Library is the Internet Archive's book lending and cataloging project, offering free access to millions of scanned public-domain books and a controlled digital lending program for in-copyright works. It is a non-profit digital preservation institution holding deep collections on Cold War history, surveillance-state history, and political theory. Public-domain collections remain fully open.
