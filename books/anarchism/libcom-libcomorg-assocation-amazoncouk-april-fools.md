Submitted by libcom on April 1, 2007 We're now working in Association with Amazon.co.uk . This means we'll have some great special offers available exclusively for our users. As part of our affiliation, Amazon will monitor which pages each user views as they browse the site, and will then offer them personalised recomendations from their wide selection of books, music and DVDs. We hope you'll enjoy these new features, and look forward to a bright future for our mutually beneficial partnership.

So a big thanks to our new friends at Amazon , and long may our relationship continue!
