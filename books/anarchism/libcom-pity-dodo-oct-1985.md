October 24th 1985 The day they closed Corton Wood - GOD BLESS THE ALAMO

Is it over then, the Struggle Have they won, and have we lost Is the final outcome what we should expect Shall we ‘wake up’ in a year or so And realise the cost When it’s much too late to save our self respect

Should we ‘give the ghost up gracefully’ And slowly fade away Should we disappear Like out of date machines? Well, I tell you If you feel like I do ‘That’ll be the day’ You can’t make Dodos Out of Human Beings
