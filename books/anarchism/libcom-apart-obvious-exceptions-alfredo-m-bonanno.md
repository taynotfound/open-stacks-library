One of the best articulators of the insurrectionist tendency on the topics of what it means to be an anarchist, and how anarchists best deal with prison, and the courts.

Bonanno has made a name for himself in being one of the best articulators of the insurrectionist tendency in Italy, a tendency that has been inspirational to a whole generation. These essays continue his thinking on the crucial topics of what it means to be an anarchist, and how anarchists best deal with prison, and the courts.
