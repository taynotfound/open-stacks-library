"What is the Workers Solidarity Alliance?" is a 1980's folded leaflet/brochure issued by the Workers Solidarity Alliance (WSA).

The leaflet / brochure was a mainstay for many years, primarily used by the New York / New Jersey Group. Additionally, the leaflet/brochure was sent to prospective new members as well (when the national office functions were in NY).
