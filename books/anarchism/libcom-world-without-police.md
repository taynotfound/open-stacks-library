Police officers arrest a demonstrator during a march held for Kimani “Kiki” Gray A pamphlet stressing the need to abolish police as an institution which provides some practical steps for communities to take in that process.

We live in a society where almost every social problem--from noisy neighbors to broken taillights--has become a point of police intervention. The result is an epidemic of harassment and violence. But what if we found other ways to solve our issues? What if we rolled back police power, and abolished the institution entirely? Here you are invited to think and act with other visionaries, and find ways to achieve a police-free world.
