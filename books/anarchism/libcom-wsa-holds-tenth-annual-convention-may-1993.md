Press release issued by the Workers Solidarity Alliance (WSA), July 1993

For many years, the Workers Solidarity Alliance (W.S.A.) would issue Press Releases for publication and for general informational purposes. Often times these Press releases would be published, either in whole or part, in the anarcho-syndicalist, libertarian socialist and anarchist press.

This PR pertains to the May 1993 W.S.A. Convention held in San Francisco, May 28-30, 1993
