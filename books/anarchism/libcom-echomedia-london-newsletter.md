Partial online archive of Echomedia London, a free anarchist / direct action newsletter established 1986. At least 15 issues were published, until 1990 or later.

Echomedia/Ecomedia newsletters were also produced in France, Germany, Spain, Canada and New Zealand.

January 1989 and April 1990 issues added courtesy of Mara Cagol Memorial Library.
