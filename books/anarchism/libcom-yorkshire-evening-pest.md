Complete online archive of Yorkshire Evening Pest, a freesheet by Leeds Class War published 1993-1994.

The title is a parody of the local newspaper Yorkshire Evening Post . Roughly four issues were produced.

With thanks to Kate Sharpley Library for providing a copy to be scanned and to Sparrows Nest Archive in Nottingham.
