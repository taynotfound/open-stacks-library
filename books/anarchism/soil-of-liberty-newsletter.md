# Soil of Liberty newsletter

Partial online archive of the newsletter published out of Minneapolis from 1975 until at least 1982 by North Country Anarchists and Anarcha-Feminists.

The last issue seems to be vol 08 #02, published Spring 1982.

Please leave a comment if you have access to any issues we are missing that could be scanned.

"Put out by North Country Anarchists and Anarcha-Feminists" Minneapolis, Minnesota, 1975

Only one issue published as Volume 1

-   S-1: Bill of Frights

-   Developing an anarcha`feminist ideology

## Soil of Liberty vol 03 #01

January 1977 issue, including: interview with hospital union activist Mark Sonnen, Nobody For President campaigns, review of Housing an Anarchist Approach by Colin Ward, The Murrays, Alexander Berkman's The Blast reappraised, letters, etc.

With thanks to Kate Sharpley Library for providing a copy to be scanned.

## Soil of Liberty vol 03 #02

Published March 1977 and including: non-violent direct action, housing struggles in Washington DC, farmer fight Minnesota power line, letters, book reviews, detourned cartoon, etc.

PDF courtesy of Spirit of Revolt Archive.

## Soil of Liberty vol 03 #05

Including: Sacco-Vanzetti 50 year memorial, long review of "Thinking Like a Woman" by Leah Fritz, Towards a free people's society in Palestine, review of "Portugal: The Impossible Revolution" by Phil Mailer, several pages of letters.

With thanks to Kate Sharpley Library for providing a copy to scan.

## Soil of Liberty vol 04 #01 & #02

Including: two articles on class conflict and energy / nuclear power, review of "The Unsettling of America: Culture and Agriculture" by Wendell Berry, Land & Revolution by Errico Malatesta, review of collected essays by Taylor Stoehr, review of "Andrew Ant the anarchist", letter on non-violence.

With thanks to Kate Sharpley Library for providing a copy to be scanned.

## Soil of Liberty vol 05 #01

Published March 1979 and including: several articles and personal accounts of opposition to the construction of a new Minnesota power line, book reviews, an exchange of letters on Sam Dolgoff's "Critique of Marxism" pamphlet, etc.

PDF courtesy of Spirit of Revolt Archive.

## Soil of Liberty vol 06 #02

Including: Conscription - a history of resistance, powerline struggles in Minnesota, account of being fired from a day care centre, book reviews (Ursula LeGuin, "The American as Anarchist", Kropotkin, Maximoff, Spanish Civil War, etc), letters on Sam Dolgoff.

With thanks to Kate Sharpley Library for providing a physical copy to scan.

## Soil of Liberty vol 08 #02

Including: Sam and Esther Dolgoff interview, Noam Chomsky on US foreign policy and the cold war, Ronald Reagan exposed poster, a commentary on Spain, book reviews (Marge Piercy and John Sayles novels on the left, Revolution in Seattle, Victor Serge).

Probably the final issue?

With thanks to Kate Sharpley Library for providing a physical copy to scan.
