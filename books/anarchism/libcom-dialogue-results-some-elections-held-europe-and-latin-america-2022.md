This dialogue started with some questions about the results of the just concluded parliamentary elections in Chile and Germany (2021), and then went on to discuss the role of elections and wars in the Aufhebung [sublation/transformation] of bürgerlichen Gesellschaft [civil society/ bourgeois society], its states/governments, and that of the idea and reality of 'left' and 'right' in politics, since the days of the first French Revolution of 1798-1791.
