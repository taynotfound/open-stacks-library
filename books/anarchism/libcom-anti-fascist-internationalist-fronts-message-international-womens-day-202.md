Revolutionary Women of Burma, and of The World, Today on International Women’s day, as on all days, we honor you.

With the fire of global struggle in our hearts, we unite our efforts with the battles and victories of all who would dismantle this femicidal dictatorship, all who strike back against the powers of fascism and capitalist modernity. Since the first wretched pillars of patriarchy were erected, the pillars on which all structures of oppression stand, women have driven and guided the revolutionary struggle to dismantle them. From the first days of the SAC Dictatorship’s war, their campaign of devastation against entire peoples, they have systematically targeted women. Their tools are not only the bomb and the bullet. Widespread, structurally enshrined rape and torture have underpinned their campaign of terror against a population who yearn for liberation. Faced with such an adversary, the women of Burma have taken up the rifle and the mortar, the slingshot and the drone to free themselves and their land from patriarchal dominion. In taking these steps, these brave bounds toward dignified freedom and autonomy, these women have retaken their historical place at the forefront of Anti-fascist resistance. In doing so, they have also necessarily taken up a second fight: the fight against the patriarchal mentality and illogic of daily life in all sections of society, and even within the revolution itself. Without Women’s Liberation, without the liberation of all oppressed by patriarchy, no society can be free. To all who fight to be free of this foundational evil, and to the martyrs who have fallen in tearing it down, we salute you.
