Photo gallery from the 1969 Cordobazo , which saw students and workers take part in a series of strikes, riots and demonstrations in a general uprising against the dictatorship of General Juan Carlos Onganía

general strikes riots uprisings Argentina Cordoba Cordobazo 1969 Comments 1 David in Atlanta

In photographs 4-6 the man center front wearing overalls was Agustín Tosco, revolutionary general secretary of the electrical utility workers union in Cordoba, who called the unions out when the police killed student demonstrators. I've never gotten a handle on his politics except that he is described as opposed to the union bureaucracy and anti-capitalist.
