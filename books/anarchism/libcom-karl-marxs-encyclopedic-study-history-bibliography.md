Political economy unfolds as a social fact inside human history. Marx’s Critique of Political Economy as a discipline has also unfolded inside that history. However, his study of history as a discipline has received less attention. Hence the need to map the trajectory of that study in the widest possible sense in which it was accessible to him. Here we present the corresponding bibliography, updated till 2023. This may serve as a repository of source materials, and as a model for the present and future investigators to emulate and develop in the interests of all round human emancipation in all societies on planet earth.
