This month there has been news from Cambodia, China, Indonesia, Japan, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, and Vietnam.

I apologise for the lateness of this summary, I was on holiday (in China in fact, so many sources were blocked) then had some technical difficulties. In any case the most important story of last month was cover by Empire Logistics here . Also, you may want to check out the new blog by Nao here which looks like it will go into far more depth than my superficial summaries of corporate media coverage.
