# CrimethInc. Ex-Workers Collective

**Source:** CrimethInc.

A prolific anarchist publishing and propaganda collective producing zines, books, posters, and long-form analysis since the late 1990s. CrimethInc. is known for accessible, visually striking materials on direct action, anti-capitalism, feminism, anti-fascism, and anarchist strategy. Their free online library and shop are widely used entry points into anarchist politics globally.
