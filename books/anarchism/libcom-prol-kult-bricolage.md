Submitted by AngryWorkersWorld on October 5, 2019 Hello comrades,

We’ve entered the culture war and would like to thank the London Arts Council and Momentum’s ‘Activist Art for Socialist Realism’-Fund for the financial support. We would also like to thank Tesco for three years of employment and the ‘Driver Handbook’ that we subversively deconstructed for our collage. Thank you all.

Most of the fellow workers are post-colonial or EU neo-colonial subjects (Hardie, 2006) and right old blockheads. Tesco has to give them some good lecturing in emotional labour and caring skills. In the end we are all up-rooted and pasted into unknown environs, where we have to pick up the right social clues and shopping trays.

Find the bricolage on our site: https://angryworkersworld.wordpress.com/2019/10/04/a-prol-kult-bricolage/
