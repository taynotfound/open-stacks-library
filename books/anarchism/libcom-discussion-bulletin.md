Discussion Bulletin 1995 70 Mar Apr Online archive of Discussion Bulletin, a quarterly journal based in Grand Rapids, Michigan. The bulletin was edited by Frank Girard and published quarterly from 1983 to 2003.

See also the publication titled The Libertarian Communist Discussion Bulletin (2009-2015).

A hodgepodge newsletter of various radical socialist and libertarian socialist participants. In spite of its often time DeLeonist blah,blag and arguments, there was some interesting stuff in it.

And jondwhite for scanning and posting this "historically" valuable newsletter.

Missing issues 12, 13 (including leaflet), 38 and 59 have now been added courtesy of the excellent Splits and Fusions archive: https://splitsandfusions.wordpress.com/

It looks like Sparrows Nest archive has issues 1-10 so perhaps they will be scanned eventually.
