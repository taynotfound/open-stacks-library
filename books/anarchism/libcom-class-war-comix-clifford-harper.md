Class War Comix The first chapter of a projected graphic novel by the English cartoonist and anarchist, published in 1979.

Set in a post-"revolution" future, the book deals with the ups and downs (mostly downs, it seems) of communal life, raising doubts about the viability of "back to the land" solutions to social problems. Originally intended as a six issue series, but never got past the first one. Written and illustrated by Clifford Harper.
