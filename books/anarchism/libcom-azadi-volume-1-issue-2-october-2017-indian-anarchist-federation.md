Azadi, meaning freedom and liberty, is a 4 page newsletter by Indian Anarchist Federation. Published and distributed primarily in city of Bhopal. It began in 2017.

First Issue of the newsletter covered India-China standoff and criticized Indian media and military institutions. It was a 2 pages document. The second issue contains discussion on Rohingya refugees and Post-Job society in India. It also looks back at the historic Kamani Tube workers take over of factory and enterprise.
