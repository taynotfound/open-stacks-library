Tribune Magazine from Iran is edited by Azar Majedi, wife of Mansoor Hekmat, an Iranian local Marxist thinker as well as a politician who developed a workerist and left-communist ideology tailored for Iranian politics and others.

I will keep updating the list with English version of the Tribune and Farsi version of Tribune.

Why Tribune? The brutal genocide committed by Israel and its western allies, led by United States in Gaza has increasedthe risk of war and destruction in the region. The possibility of a military attack on Iran is of concern. These factors and the different viewpoints of the Left, labour and communist movements in viewing these huge historical events, led us to publish the “Tribune” magazine. We rely on the theoretical achievements of the labour communism movement of Mansour Hekmat, to offer an active theoretical intervention in the course of the world's dangerous situation and Iran's sensitive situation by presenting communist analysis and solutions.
