# World Labour News (1960s)

Complete online archive of World Labour News , the English paper of the International Working Mens Association (i.e. the IWA, the syndicalist international). Eighteen issues were published bi-monthly from 1960-1962.

From January 1963, World Labour News was incorporated into the Syndicalist Workers Federation paper Direct Action .

PDFs courtesy of Sparrows Nest Archive, Splits and Fusions Archive and the personal collection of a Libcom contributor.

## World Labour News vol 1 #1  Jan 1960

Debut issue of World Labour News, including: global reports on strikes, elections in Britain, UK strike round up, the fight against Franco, victimisation of German syndicalist, etc.

PDF courtesy of Sparrows Nest Archive, Nottingham.
