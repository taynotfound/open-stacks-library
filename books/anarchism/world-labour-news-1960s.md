# World Labour News (1960s)


Complete online archive of World Labour News, the English paper of the International Working Mens Association (i.e. the IWA, the syndicalist international). Eighteen issues were published bi-monthly from 1960-1962. From January 1963, World Labour News was incorporated into the Syndicalist Workers Federation paper Direct Action.
