ABSTRACT: Cyril Smith (1929-2008) https://www.thehobgoblin.co.uk/journal/2008_12_DB_CyrilSmith.htm wrote an article on Friedrich Engels' relationship with and editorial interventions on Karl Marx's critique of political economy, in 1997.

Here, that article is supplemented with some notes, an afterword and a bibliography related to edition studies, Marx/Engels studies and science studies, as on 2023. We hope to stimulate further investigations on Marx's open-ended legacy for the emancipation of our societies and of the sciences evolving therein.
