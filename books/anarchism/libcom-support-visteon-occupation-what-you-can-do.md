A list of ways you can get involved in helping with the occupation by sacked Visteon workers.

Visit Visit the factory and demonstrate your support and keep morale high. People are welcome at any time, 12 noon is a good time to go.

It is located at Morson Road EN3 4NQ near Ponders End train station, Enfield

Donate Do a collection at work, or propose a motion to donate money at to your union branch committee. Donate to the fund which workers at the Enfield occupation control themselves here:

Account name: Haringey Solidarity Group Account number: 50642971 Sort Code: 08-90-37 Bank: Co-operative Bank Branch: Wood Green, London, N22

(This bank account will be used solely for Visteon donations during the occupation. Please put your name or group's name in the reference line.)

Or send cheques to "HSG" at PO Box 2474, London, N8 0HW. (Please write "for Visteon" on the back of cheque.)

Leaflet - Print and hand out Visteon Support leaflets to workmates, friends and neighbours. - Hand out Visteon Support leaflets at Ford dealers which represent the public face of the company. - Hand out Ford workers Visteon solidarity leaflets outside Ford plants, or to Ford workers you know - If you are in the US, hand out the US Visteon support leaflet

Stay informed Check out www.visteonoccupation.org for updates, and show it to colleagues.

+ those involved in social centres/squats/Climate Camp, they're looking to hold some sort of party/gathering onsite with a marquee, some furniture and a soundsystem.
