These are the memoirs of Vicente Ruiz Gutiérrez. His son Vicente Ruiz (hijo) edited them: "I thought I knew my father’s life, as well as the horrendous hardships he had incurred. Upon reading those pages I realised there was a lot more to his life." And we have to be grateful that they weren't destroyed:

"When I accidentally discovered what [my mother] was doing, due to the smell of burnt paper in the house, she responded by saying that the documents had to be destroyed because the fascists were coming." This is in Australia. Probably over fifty years after they had escaped. Fucking heartbreaking.
