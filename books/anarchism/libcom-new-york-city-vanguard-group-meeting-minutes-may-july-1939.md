New York City "Vanguard Group" Meeting Minutes (May-July 1939)

Also are recorded as joint Vanguard and Challenge group's meeting. By 1938 there was group overlap between the two groups and publications by their respective names. My read is that The Vanguard was effectively in its final days as the two groups were split and energy being put into “The Challenge.”

Photocopies from the New York Workers Solidarity Alliance archives (original copies at Tamiment Library & Robert F. Wagner Labor Archives, NYC)
