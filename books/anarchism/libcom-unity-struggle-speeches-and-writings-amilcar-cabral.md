Unity & Struggle: Speeches and Writings of Amilcar Cabral A collection of speeches and writings first published in 1979.

Amílcar Lopes da Costa Cabral was a Guinea-Bissauan and Cape Verdean agricultural engineer, intellectual, poet, theoretician and revolutionary. He is a key theoretician of the period of national liberation struggles in Africa.
