Partial online archive of Ekomedia Barcelona, a free English-language anarchist / direct action newsletter.

Ekomedia Barcelona merged with Agencia De Noticias Alternativia newsletter in late 1989.

Echomedia/Ecomedia newsletters were also produced in England, France, Germany, Canada and New Zealand.
