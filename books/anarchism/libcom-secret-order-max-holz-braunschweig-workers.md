An order from Max Hölz to workers in Braunschweig, asking them to suppoet the March Action with all military means. The text can found in Pg. 33 of this document .

The Braunschweig comrades who have been active with me in recent days are taking on the task for Braunschweig and its surrounding areas to vigorously support the action initiated in Central Germany with all military means. I call upon the comrades of the Braunschweig district to heed the battle cry and actively support the appointed representatives. Comrades, it is now a matter of victory or downfall.
