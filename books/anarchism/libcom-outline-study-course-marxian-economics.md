This guide, written in the 1930’s, may still prove useful for teaching Marxian economics. In order to make the guide accessible, many changes have been made.

Firstly, assigned page numbers are now accompanied by section references. This ensures that any student can read along, regardless of edition or language. All quotes have been updated to the Reitter edition of Capital. Many grammatical errors have been amended.

The Council Communist Collective hopes that by publishing this guide, Marxists everywhere can make full use of it.
