Aotearoa Workers Solidarity Movement (AWSM) statement on mass shooting in Christchurch.

Though full details have yet to be revealed, it is clear there have been multiple fatalities and serious injuries due to a mass shooting at two mosques in Christchurch today.

The Aotearoa Workers Solidarity Movement (AWSM) expresses sincere and heartfelt feelings of commiseration for the victims and their families and stand in support of them against the perpetrators, who are believed to be have been motivated by fascist and neo-Nazi ideology.
