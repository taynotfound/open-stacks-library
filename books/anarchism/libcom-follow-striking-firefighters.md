Submitted by jef costello on January 31, 2007 Five firemen are currently on trial accused of "violence against police agents with the use of or menace of a projectile weapon". There has been a demonstration of support by other firefighters. The clashes occured last November . 30 CRS were reported injured after they attempted to break up a demonstration by striking firefighters. The prosecution has called for custodial sentences for the five men, all of whom deny the charges. The Officers on whose behalf the prosecution has been brought failed to positively identify any of the men on trial as having directly injured them. The CRS commander has admitted that due to the crowd they could not arrest "whoever we wanted".
