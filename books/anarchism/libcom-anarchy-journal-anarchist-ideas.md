After it ceased publication, there was a second series of Anarchy magazine published later on by the Anarchy Collective.

Back in the day I was involved in a collaborative effort involving mostly SF and ACF members producing a freesheet of the same name. I suspect the older members knew that we weren't the first to call a publication 'Anarchy' but it was a nail-biting discussion that settled on this name. It wasn't until years later that I come across the one printed at FP, and wished we had opted on the another name we almost chose 'Strike!' - then I found out there was another publication of the same name. Anyhow, the original was best! Here's the last remnant that I can find of our freesheet: http://www.spunk.org/texts/intro/sp001632/libword.html
