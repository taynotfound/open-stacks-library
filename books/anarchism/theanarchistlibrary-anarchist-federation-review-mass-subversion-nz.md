Welcome to the first edition of Mass Subversion . This magazine is very close to the politics of the ACF and contains articles on nationalism-on the new set-up in South Africa and the sovereignty issue in New Zealand (which we intend to reprint in Organise! ) As Mass Subversion says: “The poison that is nationalism is cynically used not only by the established States but also by the national liberation movements. Just as the established State will use ‘love of one’s country’ as a powerful component to protect the interests of its’ boss clas afainst those of a foreign boss class, so also do the rising boss-class-to-be of the national liberation movements use ‘love of the people’ to clothe their power struggle. In either case, working class blood is spilt in the services of one faction of the boss class against another. Libertarian communists refuse to thake the side of any of the bosses and we expose these machinations of death and dead-ends for what they are”.

Organise! congratulates Mass Subversion on its appearance and hopes that many more issues appear and that it can be a instrument in helping build a libertarian communist movement in New Zealand and the development of a libertarian communist international. A couple of NZ dollars by international money order should obtain this mag for you at above address.
