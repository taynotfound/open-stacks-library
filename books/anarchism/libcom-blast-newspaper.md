Complete online archive of The Blast, an anarchist newspaper published by Alex Berkman for 29 issues 1916-1917, when it was shut down for encouraging resistance to the draft.

A hardcopy anthology of The Blast has been published by AK Press with an expansive introduction by Barry Pateman. We encourage you to buy this from AK UK , AK USA or elsewhere.
