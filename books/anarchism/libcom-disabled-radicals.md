[Read on libcom.org](https://libcom.org/article/disabled-radicals)

Disabled people have been active parts of every society and liberatory movement. Yet when history is written, disability is often erased or stigmatised. This zine aims to celebrate the lives of disabled radicals. To frame disability not as an individual shameful failure that undermines one’s agency, legitimacy and personhood, but as a neutral characteristic within the natural variation of humanity.
