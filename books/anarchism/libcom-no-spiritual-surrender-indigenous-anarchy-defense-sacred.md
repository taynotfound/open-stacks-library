Our ceremonial offerings of hopelessness become the destruction of faith in the transformation of a societal order manufactured on our destruction, in other words, a degeneration of settler colonial progress. The most bitter medicines are the strongest. We end despair by ending what is causing our suffering: destroying what destroys us. After all, anti-colonial means anti-settler society.
