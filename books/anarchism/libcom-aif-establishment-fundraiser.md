This is the statement from an internationalist Myanmar resistence group named "Anti-Fascist Internationalist Front" released on 7th November 2024 regarding the AIF Establishment Fundraiser.

Contact Details for Anti-Fascist Internationalist Front are as follow: Anti-Fascist Internationalist Front 26-10-2024 [email protected]

To our comrades and supporters, Just a short while ago we sent out the announcement regarding the formation of the AIF, and already many of you have contacted us with messages of support. As we prepare for the first groups of comrades to join us in Myanmar, we are holding this fundraiser to cover the initial costs of our operations. Without working closely with our local comrades and allies, the AIF would not be possible. That being said, it is our goal as internationalists to reduce our burden on the revolution and our financial and logistical draw upon the already-strained resistance. As many in the Myanmar diaspora are organized in support of the armies fighting for the liberation of the country, we too call upon our internationalist friends and comrades to support our frontline against the dictatorship. Our initial goal is $10,000, which will be put to use covering the fundamentals of our operations: most urgently, a vehicle. Further, while our comrades are completely self-financed, this money will allow us to provide equipment to comrades who do not have it and cover any other expenses which may arise in the short term. The difficult nature of the revolution in Myanmar means that even the most basic supplies and equipment are considerably more expensive. We hope that you will contribute, share it as wide as you are able, and contact us directly if you have any proposals regarding the fundraiser. Until victory!
