Charlie Bauer in the 80s Charlie Bauer, ultra-left anarchist sympathiser, former friend and associate of Jacques Mesrine, died of a heart attack on Sunday, 7th August, at the age of 68.

Knowing something of his life of stubborn resistance to this society, and having briefly met him just over a year ago, this news I've only just heard about sends shivers through my body. Sad. And so young, as well.
