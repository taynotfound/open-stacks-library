1978 - mid-1980s Photos of members of the Libertarian Workers Group and the New York Workers Solidarity Alliance.

Members and others on various picket lines, on marches and in support of the Direct Action Movement's support campaign on behalf Laura Ashley garment workers at Ardbride factories in Ardrossan, Scotland. Needle Trades Action Committee members showing signs off in the old WSA office, before heading off to a picket in support of Polish workers.
