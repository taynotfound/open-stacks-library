Special covert edition of Situationniste International #10. Text below from Situationniste Blog : Tenth issue of Internationale Situationniste. This was one of only two issues (the other being #12 1 ) to have some copies bound in plain wrappers instead of the typical glossy, SI-branded wrappers. Such precautions were taken to facilitate clandestine distribution in countries where the S.I.’s subversive political writings may have elicited an undue amount of interest from the authorities.
