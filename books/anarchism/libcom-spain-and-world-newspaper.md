The entire set is here, starting with the December 1936 issue (1:1) and going to the end of 1938 when ti switched to Revolt (which is also there).

(As a note, the August final issue of Freedom just before SatW starts sort of is, sort of isn't a "legit" final issue, inasmuch as it's essentially just a vehicle for the CNT but was sorted out by the Freedom Publishing Committee led by Ralph Barr. Barr had come down on the Tom Keell side of the split with George Cores and was subsequently a key figure in getting everyone to stop doing their own thing and put effort behind Spain and The World).
