Workers strike at an automotive parts factory in Wuhan, China Summary and links to news stories of workers' struggles around East Asia during August 2012 and related resources. The most important stories appear on my Twitter feed as soon as I find them: http://twitter.com/spartacusnews.

This month there has been news from Burma/Myanmar, Cambodia, China, Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, and Vietnam.

Starting this month I have also started adding information about struggles outside of East Asia that I come across - I haven't filtered these as much or attempted to refine them into categories as I have started to do with the ones in the summary, but they might be a starting point for others. Though the design of the page is still pretty basic, you can view them here . If anyone would like to compile similar summaries for other regions using that website, get in touch. It is still very much in development though...
