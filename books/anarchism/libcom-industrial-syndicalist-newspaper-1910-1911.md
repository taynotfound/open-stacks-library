Anthology of The Industrial Syndicalist, published by Guy Bowman and Tom Mann in the UK from 1910-1911. Includes an introduction by Geoff Brown.

Libcom note: Tom Mann is a controversial figure in the workers' movement, not least because of his lengthy support for Stalinism later in his life.

This anthology was published in book form in 1974. Several pages in the centre of this book are blank (p274/275, 278/279, 282/283, 286/287).
