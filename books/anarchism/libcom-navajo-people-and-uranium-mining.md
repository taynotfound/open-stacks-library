A collection of oral histories and other documents relating to the harmful and exploitative history of uranium mining on the Navajo Nation from the 1940s to the 1980s. Uranium-mine operators contracted with the U.S. government in order to extract uranium from Navajo lands, mostly for nuclear-weapons production during the Cold War.
