[Video] Interview on Iranian Revolution 2022 with Azar Majedi

Hein Htet Kyaw (Abu Bakr) interviewed Azar Majedi, the chairperson of the Organization for Women's Liberation on the Iranian revolution.

There are four episodes in this discussion. 1) Intro to "Workers Communism" (an ideology founded by Mansoor Hekmat) 2) Previous Revolutions in Iran 3) Neither Shah nor Mullah 4) The possibilities of armed struggle in Iran

Episode (1) - Intro to "Workers Communism" (an ideology founded by Mansoor Hekmat) In this episode, a Iranian local Marxist tendency called worker-communism is being discussed. The differences between worker-communism with other mainstream communist tendencies such as Stalinism, Maoism and Trotskyism are discussed to point out the essences of worker-communism.

Episode (2) - Previous Revolutions in Iran In this episode, she described how previous revolutions were hijacked and how previous revolutions are stay playing a role in current protests.

Episode (3) - Neither Shah nor Mullah In this episode, she described how the underreported workers' movement in Iran are developing. There is an underreported slogan among the Iranian workers which say "No more Kings, no Mullah! All Powers to the Shuras (Soviet Councils)!"

Episode (4) - The possibilities of armed struggle in Iran In this episode, she described if the possibilities of armed struggle in Iran is possible or not.
