Important book on money and debt edited by Open Marxists Werner Bonefeld and John Holloway. Released in 1996 with contributions from Christian Marazzi, Peter Burnham, Harry Cleaver, Bonefeld and Holloway.

The central theme of the book is the limitations and constraints on state action which arise from the relation between the (nation) state and the global flow of money.
