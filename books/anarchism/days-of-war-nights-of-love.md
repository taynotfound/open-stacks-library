# Days of War, Nights of Love

**Source:** CrimethInc.

A landmark CrimethInc. publication combining manifesto, art, and poetry to make the case for anarchist and anti-capitalist living. Originally self-published as a zine-format book, it became one of the most widely distributed anarchist texts of the 2000s. It is freely readable online and representative of CrimethInc.'s accessible, culturally engaged approach to radical politics.
