June 1983 Libertarian Workers Group leaflet against the Shoreham, Long Island, New York LILCO nuclear power plant

Libertarian Workers Group leaflet against LILCO nuclear power plant. Linking the struggle against nuclear power and nuclear weapons. Calling upon the anti-nuclear power movement to get back to direct action tactics and against reliance on politicians.
