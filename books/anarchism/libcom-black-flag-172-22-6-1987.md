An issue of class struggle anarchist newspaper Black Flag, including UK and international news, workfare, investigation into Western Goals UK and its links to the Conservative Party, Economic League fearful of strike support groups, Roberto Fiore and the National Front, etc.

Page 4 of this issue features an incredible photo of the Director of the Economic League brandishing a copy of the insurrectionist "Flamethrower" paper, backed by a wild array of far left and far right publications including Wildcat and erm, Labour Briefing.

It's page 5, but that's fantastic! Does anyone have any physical copies of Flamethrower? Can't seem to find any online

I've got a tatty one, but haven't scanned it in because it was mainly nonsense iirc. (Lots of ALF stuff). We do have the critique of Class War by "Julian" in the library, which was the best bit in it: https://libcom.org/article/view-class-war-former-member-julian-1986 .
