By the Iraqi Council Communist Fouad Mohammed Mahmoud, this text serves as an attack on Leninism. Originally published in "Al-Hewar Al-Mutamadin, 22nd November 2012"

A call to remove the names of Lenin, Trotsky and Stalin from the flags of Marxism and put them on trial.

After the fall of the Leninist program, events proved that the Leninist program did not lead to socialism and the losses suffered by the peoples, especially the Soviet people, as a result of the Leninist program that deviated from Marxism, which they falsely called Marxism.

Leninism delayed the victory of communism, increased the power of capitalism and overcame its crises, delayed and distorted revolutions, and missed many opportunities for the victory of communism.

It was the main reason for the splits in the communist movement, the death of the revolutionary Guevara, the abortion of the communal council revolutions, and the deviation of socialist revolutions into state capitalism.

It also worked to turn Marxism into rigid texts and perpetuate many deviations, such as replacing the party in place of the working class, abolishing democracy and pluralism, installing the dictatorship of the first secretary, deviating to the right, and bargaining with capitalism in dividing the world, as it was confined within Russia and betrayed the world revolution.

The crimes committed under Stalin played a major role in tarnishing Marxism.

He dissolved the Third International and made the Communist Parties his tails. In show trials, he executed more than 20 million Soviet citizens, including most of the comrades in the party leadership.

Workers, laborers, intellectuals and communists of the world, unite!
