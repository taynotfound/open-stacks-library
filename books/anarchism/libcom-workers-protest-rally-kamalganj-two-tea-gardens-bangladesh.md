Workers' protest rally in Kamalganj two tea gardens, Bangladesh

There is no need for traditional political parties to get their rights. Kamalgonj Tea workers have proved their activities only if they are conscious of their own right and without being a member of the most pretentious political party. March 30, 2018 was a special day for their demanding movement.

In the third round of protests against the owner of the workers in making wages agreement with the tea laborers, the workers in Kalma and Chambera tea garden of Kamalganj upazila observed the last day's demonstration and two-hour strike. Later, tea workers protested and protested in front of the main gate of two tea gardens. The tea workers held a protest meeting from 9am to 11pm on Friday morning.

In front of the main gate of Kurma tea garden factory, in front of the main gate of the garden panchayat president Narad Pati chaired a protest meeting; Manu Dhalai Valley (region) president Gopal Nunia, vice president Gayetri Rani Rajver, Baghachra tea garden panchayat president Rakhal Goala, former UP Member Nuru Haque, women's member Krishna Kawest, laborer Sardar Balak Das Pineka and labor leader Raji Kairy. At the same time, in the meeting with the president of the garden panchayat president Shankar Bunnery in front of tea garden main chamber in Chambera, in the protest meeting, Tea Workers said, "Tea workers are NOT happy, Lashmani kari, Laksmi Alikk, Mukta Alikk."
