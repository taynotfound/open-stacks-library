Miscellaneous Local New York - New Jersey Workers Solidarity Alliance leaflets. the Local later became known as Metro NY WSA. This Local grew out of one of the founding WSA-IWA Locals, the Libertarian Workers Group (LWG). While never more than a dozen local members at any one time, the Local was engaged in many activities, demonstrations, campaigns and actions, the leaflets are reflective of those activities.
