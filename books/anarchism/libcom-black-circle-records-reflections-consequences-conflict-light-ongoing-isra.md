Black Circle Records have released a compilation of underground noise/electronic/abstract/weirdo sounds reflecting on the Israeli states current offensive in Gaza.

"This album protests Israel's actions in the West Bank where international observers have claimed that they are deliberately targeting civilians.

This album also protests the continued use of terror amongst civilian populations as a weapon of war and sees Israel's actions as part of this pattern.

All artists on this album see no place for anti-Semitism in protesting Israel's actions in Gaza."

The compilation contains music by myself , Elizabeth Veldon , Concrete/Field and Jennifer Fullerton among others.

I want to ask why is the world silent now? Why is America and other powerful states are silent and letting Israel run the massacre?
