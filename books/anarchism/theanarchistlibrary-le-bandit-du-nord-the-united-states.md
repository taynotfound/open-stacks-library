It’s not only France and the other governments of Europe that pursue us. On November 11, 1887 the Americans hung four of ours in Chicago. The day of the execution comrade Most publicly condemned the bourgeoisie and as a result was sentenced to prison. He has just been arrested and is serving his sentence.

Bravo, gentlemen of the bourgeoisie. The more you strike out at our ideas the more they will spread. Strike hard!
