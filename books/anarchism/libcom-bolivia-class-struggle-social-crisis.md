Pamphlet looking at the class struggle in Bolivia underlying the struggle between Evo Morales and the oligarchy.

The Commune 's pamphlet on the class struggle and right-wing coup attempts in Bolivia includes the following articles and documents:

- Evo Morales, the Bolivian oligarchy and the workers' movement, by David Broder - Central Obrera [union federation]: 'neither Evo nor the oligarchy' - Political Declaration of the Federación Sindical de Trabajadores Mineros de Bolivia [the miners' union] - Statement by the Huanuni tin miners - Central Obrera Boliviana call truce with Morales - Fascists attempt to split Bolivia - Coup threat in Bolivia - Morales opens negotiations with the fascists
