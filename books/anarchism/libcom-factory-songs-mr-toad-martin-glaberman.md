In addition to his political writings, Martin Glaberman was also a published poet. Here is an example of his work.

Walking silently on padded feet Unseen, unheard Power concentrated in a compact body

Lean, lithe, less in appearance Than the explosive leap, periodic culmination of growing power of growing hunger

Amber, black, mottled, gold. All colors help to hide its invisible path

All right, Buddy Let's not get romantic. Shut her down and let's go A most practical cat
