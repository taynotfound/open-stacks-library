One-off anarchist/communist publication from 1990, with articles on rank and filism, the government's Enterprise Allowance Scheme, Ireland and fragmentation.

Libcom note: The title and logo of this are a parody of Exchange & Mart , a well known UK magazine largely consisting of classified adverts (for people buying and selling cars etc). It was published weekly from 1868 to 2009. PDF courtesy of Sparrows Nest Archive, Nottingham.
