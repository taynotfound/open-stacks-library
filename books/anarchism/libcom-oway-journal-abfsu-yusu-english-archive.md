The Original "Oway" magazine was first published by the historic YUSU (Yangon University Student Union) since 1936, led by Thakin Aung San, Thakin Mya, and Thakin Kyaw Nyein.

In 2020, contemporary YUSU reintroduced its own "Oway Journal" as its publication along with ABFSU (All Burma Federation of Student Unions).

Oway Journal Volume 4, Issue 2 which was released on May is the first English publication of Oway.

Archivist Note Since Oway is available in English, I'm archiving it as it indeed hold syndicalist values such as upholding the interests of students and their adjacent solidarity with the interests of workers, and peasants. While its syndicalism is not limited to trade unionism, but also extends to syndicalism of any kind including national syndicalism. Also, it’s important to note that a significant fraction of ABFSU plays a vital role in either advocating for Rohingya genocide or genocide apologism. However, most commonly, you will mostly see the anti-military junta theme as it's the immediate goal at this point in history.
