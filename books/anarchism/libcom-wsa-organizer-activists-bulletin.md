First issue of the [WSA] “ORGANIZERS & ACTIVISTS BULLETIN”. The purpose of the Bulletin was to aid and assist W.S.A. members in their efforts at organizing their co-workers, either in their individual place of work or in their effort to organize a particular “industry”.

This effort was not intended to to replace or surpass the printed monthly internal Discussion Bulletin or the published magazine ideas & action.

The first (and only)issue of “Organizers & Activists Bulletin”, July 1989. Internally distributed to members of the Workers Solidarity Alliance (WSA) was an effort to aid and assist worker militants/activists in their efforts at shop floor or broader “industry”organizing. The aim was to offer technical assistance regarding publishing leaflets, shop newsletters and other technical advice and assistance.

The “Bulletin” turned out to be a one-off publication. Further shop floor discussion, strategies, tactics and activities were to be confined to the internal Discussion Bulletin for the time being. The effort was not intended to to replace or surpass the printed monthly internal Discussion Bulletin or the published magazine ideas & action.

Produced and distributed by under WSA New York name. (“Ghost” written and produced by Mike Harris).
