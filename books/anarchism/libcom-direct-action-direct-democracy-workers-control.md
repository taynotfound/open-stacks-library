Late 1980s - late 1990s two sided leaflet recruitment leaflet.

Side one, the graphic as shown. Side one text taken from Issue #16 of the then WSA printed magazine "ideas & action".

"Direct Action - Direct Democracy - Workers Control" was a general local and national WSA two sided recruitment leaflet. Primarily used when responding to correspondence and various contacts. Used from the late 1980s to the late 1990s.

Side one text taken from Issue #16 of the then WSA printed magazine "ideas & action".
