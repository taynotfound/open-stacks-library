Ever since the ’70s, politically speaking, the race was disconnected from class. The general idea that in the Land of Opportunities you could make it, regardless of race, gender, religion, and what not, became prevalent, as a direct result of the New Deal hegemony. Constantly referring to race and gender while overlooking class is liberal identity politics and also wrong. Let's try intersectionality for a change, it might work for us. Otherwise, we can’t see the forest for the trees.
