[Read on libcom.org](https://libcom.org/article/disabled-gods)

Disabled Gods offers a glimpse of how disability was thought of in the ancient past. This is not suitable for children, as it contains nudity, infanticide, human sacrifice, violence, murder, ableism, and infantilisation.

In a world where disability is often erased from histories, learning about disabled gods does at least remind us that disabled people have always existed and impacted the storytelling and culture in all the places they lived.
