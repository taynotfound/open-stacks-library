Many prominent people with dwarfism have existed throughout history. Some held high positions within the royal courts of ancient Egypt, while others were hidden away, enslaved or dismissed. The degree of exclusion and prejudice that people with dwarfism currently experience is neither natural nor universal, as inclusion and acceptance has varied greatly over time and between cultures.
