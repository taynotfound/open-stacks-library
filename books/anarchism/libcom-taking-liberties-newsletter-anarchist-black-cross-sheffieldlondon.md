Partial online archive of Taking Liberties , a 1990s newsletter of the Anarchist Black Cross covering prisons, prisoner solidarity, miscarriages of justice etc. Originally produced in Sheffield, later issues in London.

Taking Liberties #11 announced that it was now incorporating the London ABC bulletin .

Issues 8-12 & 19 taken from Sparrows Nest Archive, Nottingham UK. Issue 13 taken from Fundación Anselmo Lorenzo (FAL) archive, Madrid - Yuncler (Toledo), Spain. Issue 15 taken from Arm The Spirit archive, Toronto, Canada.

With thanks to Kate Sharpley Library for providing physical copies of other issues to be scanned.

Number 2 is may/jul.1991 produced in Sheffield so No 1 sometime just before that.
