The Ryanair Don't Care campaign is calling for a week of action against exploitation and recruitment-scamming by Ryanair starting on March 12th 2012.

In particular, the campaign is asking for actions (pickets, occupations, leafletting etc) to take place on March 15th, which is the day of the Ryanair Chase at Cheltenham Races, where actions are planned.

The campaign will also be calling on the public to boycott the airline for the duration of the week of action.

Liverpool Solidarity Federation is backing the call-out and the campaign hopes members of the International Workers' Association across Europe, as well as trade unions and like-minded groups will join in as well.

Liverpool Solidarity Federation statement here . Watch John's New Year message on YouTube here .

Previous actions... Ryanair Don't Care and Liverpool SolFed protest at Liverpool John Lennon Airport. Picket by the CNT's Ryanair section. Protest by the CNT's Ryanair section.
