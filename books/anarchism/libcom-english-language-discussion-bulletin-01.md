Just as a point of clarification, the ELDB was a collaborative project of the Direct Action Movement (DAM), the Anarcho-Syndicalist Federation (Australia, ASF) and the Workers Solidarity Alliance (WSA). This was not issued by the IWA Secretariat. The aim was really to build closer relations, cooperation and solidarity between english speaking IWA Section's. As a side note, as much as the CNT based IWA Secretariat tried to do bi-lingual bulletins, the english language translations were very rough and hard to read. The early 1990s German-based Secretariat was excellent with bi-lingual stuff. And they were really the first post WWII Secretariat who were fluent in english and spanish.
