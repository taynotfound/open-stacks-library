Dear brother you spoke so plain children listened to your song of freedom played in games, stories and life

brother you danced so lightly you whistled as you soared over prison walls and tombs

dear brother your spirit sings songs of freedom wrenched from slaver’s cruelty

you leave us your tunes swinging blues rocking rap brass staccatos peace by piece a revolution riff
