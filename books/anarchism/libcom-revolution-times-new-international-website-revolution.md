Introduction to revolutiontimes.world, a collectively developed website for the revolution

https://revolutiontimes.world We are for revolution without borders, through a generalized insurrection against all states, against capital and property, through the abolition of commodities, money, work, family… The creation of a new world free from exploitation and oppression cannot be separated from the destruction of existing society.

Within uprisings around the world, autonomous dynamics are concretely raising the question of revolution in their practices. We are part of this endeavor. We are linked to a history, that of the revolutionary class struggle.

In the current period, our class is struggling and fighting. How do we get to world revolution? This site is a tool for forging links, meeting, and participating, along with others, in this global collective effort to pierce the fog of the present.

* Report on the uprisings currently taking place. * Work to build a collective vision of the revolution and its victory. * Promote the real struggles of our class in everyday life.

Here you will find texts written collectively by the editorial team. A short text entitled “What do we want?” presents a number of tasks that the collective behind this site wishes to carry out.

You will also find what constitutes the common foundation of our political positions, our relationship to struggles and their dynamics, an analysis of the period, as well as an attempt at a world map of uprisings and a list of questions that we wish to share with comrades in struggle around the world. Other texts will follow.

We have chosen a collective writing process, which takes time. We will make a special effort to translate the texts into as many languages as possible, which we believe is essential in an internationalist approach. Any help in this regard will be welcome.
