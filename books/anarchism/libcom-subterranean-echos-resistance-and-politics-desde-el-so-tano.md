Raúl Zibechi Raul Zibechi on the new forms of struggle and organisation emerging in the urban peripheries of Latin American cites.

The distance between the old worker and unionist movement and the current players has two distinct dimensions: the relationship to territory and the relationship to reproduction. The first entails a progression from external control (heteronomı´a) to relative autonomy, evidenced in the insurrectionary disposition. The second, intimately connected to the former, involves a transition on the part of the subjects from dependence on capital to control of production and reproduction of their living conditions.
