The 6 November 1846 issue of the Voice of Industry (Vol. 2 No. 21). Contents mostly consist of various responses from Congressional candidates who were answering a circular letter put out by a committee of the Industrial Reform Association of Lowell, whose members were associated with the Voice . The letter inquired about the candidates' position with respect to labor reform and a host of other issues.
