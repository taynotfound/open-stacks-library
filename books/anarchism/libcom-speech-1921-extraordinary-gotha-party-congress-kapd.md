English translation of the speech delivered by KAPD leader Karl Schröder at the 1921 Extraordinary Gotha Party Congress of the KAPD with a "Foreword" by me.

Delivered on February 16, 1921 at the Volkhaus in Gotha, this speech touches on the several tasks that were confronting the KAPD at that time, especially it's position on Trade Unions, the 3rd International, General Workers' Union [AAU] and the Party-form.
