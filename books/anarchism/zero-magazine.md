# Zero magazine

An archive of the London-based magazine anarchist / anarcha-feminist magazine Zero from the late 1970s.

Taken from Anarchy Archives .

## Zero magazine issue 1 June 1977

Including: spanish anarchists re-organise, Charter 77, anarchism/feminism, Mujeres Libres, CNT interview, Marie Louise Berneri, reviews, listings, etc.

## Zero magazine issue 2 August 1977

Including: against rape, Grunwicks strike, CNT organises, youth revolt in Italy, reflections on Oz Magazine and the counterculture, news, reviews, letters, listings, etc.

## Zero magazine issue 3 October 1977

Including: anti-nukes protests, feminism and the Italian left, gays under attack, George Woodcock on anarchism's failures, news, reviews, listings, etc.

Whilst generally good, this issue is marred by a shamefully positive piece on P.I.E. (Paedophile Information Exchange) which passes without comment in this, or later issues.

This article is included for information and completeness (and as a warning about the sort of shit that was tolerated by the 1970s "counter culture"). It's not endorsed by me or Libcom.

## Zero magazine issue 4 December 1977

Including: Red Army Faction and government repression in West Germany, interview with Yu Shuet a disillusioned former Red Guard, news, reviews, letters, listing, etc.

## Zero magazine issue 5 February 1978

Including: direct action against Fleet Street homophobia, "draft theses for principles of revolutionary unity" by Anti-Authoritarian Revolutionary Movement, Gay Liberation Front conference, news, letters, etc.

## Zero magazine issue 6 May 1978

Including: ABC trial, anarchist May Day, imprisonment of Petra Krause, repression in Greece, interview with former Red Guards, news, reviews, listings, etc.

## Zero magazine issue 7 August 1978

Including: London anarchists arrested (would become "Persons Unknown" trial), farmers and students in Japan in bloody fight against new airport, personal politics and freedom, a report from Italy, listings, news, etc.
