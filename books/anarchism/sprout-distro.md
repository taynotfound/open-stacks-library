# Sprout Distro

**Source:** Sprout Distro

A long-running anarchist zine and pamphlet distro offering free downloads of hundreds of titles covering anarchist theory, direct action, and organizing. The project prioritizes accessibility, providing print-ready PDFs at no cost. It is one of the most comprehensive free anarchist literature archives on the web.
