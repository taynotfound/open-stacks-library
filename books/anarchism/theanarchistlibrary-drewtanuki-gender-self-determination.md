I am a human. I am not defined by my genitals. Just because I have a penis, doesn’t mean that I am a “male”. Just because I have a vagina, doesn’t mean that I am a “female”. Just because I have genitals that do not align with an arbitrary binary, doesn’t mean you can force me into one of the two categories above. Why do we even have this binary? To categorize things? To get a better understanding of the world around us? To determine what people should do based on our body parts? Like any label, it can be, and has been, used to sort us. Control us. Subjugate us. Stereotype us. We’re categorized based on our body parts, our reproductive capabilities, and told we have to do these jobs... Act this way based on your body parts... Not act the way those with the “opposite” body parts... Mate with the people who have the “opposite” body parts from yours... Treat those that don’t fit in, or follow these “norms” as subhuman. As “SINFUL”. If you mate with more than one person... Love more than one person... Mate with someone with the “same” genitals... Mate with someone whose genitals are not “normal”... Love someone who doesn’t subscribe to this binary... Be someone who doesn’t subscribe to this binary... You will be shunned. Insulted. Cast off. Beaten. Murdered. Told you’re going to an eternal lake of fire in an “afterlife” where no empirical evidence of it exists. Simply because some hypocrite “G-D” says so. Simply because “His” followers want an excuse to be mean and cruel. Because they don’t want to learn. Don’t want to think there is another way. Your livelihood.... Your future.... Your very liberty.... Is determined by these arbitrary labels and “morals”. Labels imposed on us. To control us. To enslave us. To destroy us...... There is a better way. You are not defined by your genitals. You are not defined by you reproductive abilities. You can love whoever. You can mate with those who are freely willing, able, and want to mate with you. Genitals do not, and should not matter. Having a penis does not automatically make you a rapist, or that you are automatically evil. Having a vagina, uterus, and ovaries does not automatically require you to have children, or that you are automatically innocent and virginal. Having genitals and genes that do not look, function, or align with “the binary” does not automatically make you “different”. YOU ARE DEFINED BY YOUR BEHAVIOURS AND ACTIONS, NOT YOUR BIOLOGY! I refuse to be defined by my body! I practice gender self-determination. I am a gender liberationist. I have no gender. I am free of gender. I am free of “biological sex”. I am queer. I believe in liberation from “gender norms”. Liberation from gender power dynamics. Liberation from those who use gender to control, dominate, enslave, and destroy us. Liberation to love. To love others like me. I love others like me. I love others who want to be like me. I love those who I share important parts of my life with. I love those who want to share their lives with me. I love to mate. I love those who want to mate with me, and I them. I love those that don’t mate, or ever want to. I love those that want to mate with others, and respects those others. I love those who respect others’ boundaries. I am not male. I am not female. I am queer. I am a person. I am a lover. I fight for those I love. I won’t be labelled. I won’t be ‘other’ed. I won’t be denied my freedom to reject your false binary. And your false G-D, and false “sins.” I WILL BE LIBERATED!
