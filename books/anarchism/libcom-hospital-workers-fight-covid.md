This video shows a protest of health workers of the privatized Highland Hospital in Alameda County / California on October 7, 2020. https://en.labournet.tv/hospital-workers-fight-covid-privatization-bullying-union-busting Video by laborvideo

The workers try to make sure the working class community that needs the hospital is served and go to work risking an infection. In sharp contrast to this, the administration doesn't provide Personal Protection Equipment, outsources part of the workforce and targets workers who speak up about the problems.

The workers demand a re-communalization of the hospital as the management is preventing thm from providing health care for the community and is failing to protect workers and patients from Covid-19.
