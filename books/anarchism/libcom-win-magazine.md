Win Magazine was a publication produced with an attempt to build links between "left" and "right" libertarians.

Similar to " Libertarian analysis ", a publication produced out of New York during the 1970s that attempted to build links between "left" and "right" libertarians, Win Magazine was a publication produced with an attempt to build links between "left" and "right" libertarians. Although it paved the way for modern right-libertarianism and free-market ideology, the journal also served as a vital foundation for left-libertarianism, individualist anarchism, and the pacifist movement. It focused on "Peace and Freedom through Nonviolent Action".
