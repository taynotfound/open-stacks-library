Pamphlet jointly issued by the College Student Union (New Paltz, New York) and the Community Action Collective (Buffalo, New York)

Organizing pamphlet issued by SCU and CAC, in conjunction with "down state" and New Jersey students (and others) building towards a Northeastern Regional Student Union. Most of the main organizers were student syndicalists, anarchists and libertarian socialists.
