International Workers' Association 90th anniversary conference

Due to unforeseen circumstances, including one of the key organisers and speakers having been taken seriously ill, we have had to reduce the conference to just Saturday 5th January 2013. Apologies for any inconvenience this may cause.

IWA-AIT 90th anniversary conference Hosted by South London Solidarity Federation

12:00 – 13:15 IWA - 90’s years of ideas & practice For 90 years the IWA has been at the forefront of the struggle against capital and the state, a SolFed member with decades of experience as an workplace militant and long time IWA affiliate introduces the theory and practice behind anarcho-syndicalism.

14:00 – 15:15 FORA history & current activities The FORA, the Regional Workers' Federation of Argentina is one of the oldest sections of the IWA with more than 110 years of activity and a distinct organisational form. Guillermo Zunzunegui gives a talk on the history and current activities of the FOR A, with a focus on the social crisis in Argentina in 2001.

15:30 – 16:50 Priama Akcia on the IWA in Slovakia Priama akcia (PA) will speak about the experiences in the course of its development since it was founded in 2000 until now, focusing on the evolution and changes in its activities, perspectives and directions. A few words will be said about the IWA, mainly with regard to solidarity actions with the IWA sections and PA involvement in the internal life of the IWA.

PA members will then focus on the aim of the organization - helping its own members in the workplace, as well as expressing solidarity with other struggling workers. PA is also interested in hearing ideas from comrades in the audience about the problems we face in our everyday activities.

17:00 – 18:00 Solidarity Federation, past and present An ex-DAM and current SF member will look at the history of Solidarity Federation with an accompanying talk by another SF member covering the current anti-workfare campaign.

Entry is free but donations to help with the cost of organising the event would be appreciated. A social will be held in the evening.

Location: SOAS - room B102 on the first floor of the Brunei Gallery ( Google Map )
