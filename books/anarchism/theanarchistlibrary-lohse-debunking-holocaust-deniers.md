I called him out, told him he’s an antisemite, and it’s ridiculous that someone denying The Holocaust would call me, an anarchist, a fascist. A word he clearly doesn’t understand the meaning of. But there are a huge number of books and articles and utter effin’ nonsense pseudoscience they like to claim as sources for their “research”. These pricks are nothing if not committed. If you’re looking for a supposed “balanced view” where I give any of their ideas any credence, you won’t find it here. There is no balance. The Holocaust happened. It was appalling.
