On Dezember 5, 2019 in France a massive strike wave against the pension reform began.

"Nobody is going to accept this carnage", says Ahmed Berrahal, a bus driver at Flandres station in Pantin, north-east of Paris, anticipating the strike might go on for 3 weeks to a month: "We already know today that we would loose 700 Euro and would have to work 10 years longer."
