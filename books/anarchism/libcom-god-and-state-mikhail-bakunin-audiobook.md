Mikhail Bakunin by Rae Maxwell Bakunin’s most famous work, published in various lengths, this version is the most complete form of the work published hitherto, recorded as an audiobook by LibriVox .

Originally titled “Dieu et l’état”, Bakunin intended it to be part of the second portion to a larger work named “The Knouto-Germanic Empire and the Social Revolution” (Knouto-Germanic Empire is in reference to a treaty betwixt Russia and Germany at the time), but the work was never completed.

By Mikhail Bakunin (1814-1876). Translated by Benjamin R. Tucker (1854-1939). Reading by Carl Manchester. The text version is available online here .
