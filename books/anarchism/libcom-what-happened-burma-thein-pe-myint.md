This is a book by a co-founder of Communist Party of Burma who later got expelled from CBP due to his Browderist position against Maoism of CPB. The book was published around 1943 in English.

He is by no standard anarchist. He was a browderist (Marxist-Leninist) communist. I'm just archiving this book here as there aren't much books from left-wing perspective written by organic leftists from Burma in English.
