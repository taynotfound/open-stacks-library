Dwight Macdonald Fascism and the American Scene was written as the introduction to the American edition of Daniel Guerin’s Fascism and Big Business , a study of fascism in Italy and Germany. In this study of American fascism, Dwight Macdonald tries to show that the European experience can teach this country a great deal about how fascism develops and how it can—and cannot—be fought.
