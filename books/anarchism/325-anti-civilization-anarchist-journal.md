# 325 - Anti-Civilization Anarchist Journal

**Source:** 325 (nostate.net)

An anarchist journal and distro focused on anti-civilization, insurrectionary anarchism, and anti-state resistance. 325 publishes communiques, analysis, and solidarity texts from anarchist and anti-authoritarian struggles worldwide. The site archives issues of its zine alongside a broad range of related texts and translations.
