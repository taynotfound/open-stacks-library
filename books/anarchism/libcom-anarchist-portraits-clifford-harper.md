A beautiful set of 36 woodcut style portraits of anarchists by Clifford Harper, originally published as a set of picture cards by Freedom Press. If you enjoy these images please donate or buy materials from Freedom or Clifford from their websites.

Can you still buy these at an affordable price? I lost mine a few moves ago.

It would be awesome if these would be expanded with more women. Just to think of a couple of names: Voltairine de Cleyre, Milly Wittkop, Mika Feldman de Etchebéhère, Lucía Sánchez Saornil, Clara Wichmann, Itō Noe, Ida Mett, Flora Tristan, Marie Goldsmith and Ursula le Guin to name a few.
