A short 1981 pamphlet written and compiled by long time Libertarian Book Club (LBC) Member Valerio Isca about his loving comrade Ida Pilat Isca.

Valerio and Ida were mainstay's of the NYC Libertarian Book Club (LBC) for decades. Ida, always the gentle soul, was robbed of her mind by Alzheimer's. That did not stop Ida and Valerio from attending to LBC business and meetings. As Ida and Valerio had no children, Valerio became Ida's care giver until her final days.

A biographical sketch of Valerio may be found on libcom: https://libcom.org/history/valerio-isca
