The Anarchist Black Cross Bulletin was produced by London ABC in the early 1990s, covering prisoner solidarity, prisons, miscarriages of justice etc.

At least nine issues were published. The Bulletin was superceded by Taking Liberties in the mid-90s.

Issues below scanned in by the comrades at Sparrows Nest archive, Nottingham.
