Black Out: Giornale metropolitano delle lotte autonome (1978) Black Out: Giornale metropolitano delle lotte autonome ('Metropolitan Journal of Autonomous Struggles')

The first issue of Black Out: Giornale metropolitano delle lotte autonome ('Metropolitan Journal of Autonomous Struggles'), published in 1978. Internal notes state that it is a supplement to issue 24-25 of the militant journal Rosso, though there was no issue 24-25 of Rosso (there was a #23-24 and a #25-26). Emilio Vesce is named as directing the publication.

The introductory article cites inspiration from the chaotic response to the 1977 New York City blackout, and states (rough translation): "This paper was born from a discussion that territorial collectives factory and school collectives have begun and would like to continue on these pages about the situation in Milan."
