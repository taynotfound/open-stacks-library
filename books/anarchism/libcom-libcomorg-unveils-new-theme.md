This May Day 2012 we are happy to unveil the beta version of our new theme.

There are a few teething problems with it which we are aware of, and some we may not be. If you have any problems please check this thread in feedback to see if we are aware of the problem already, and if not let us know about it there.

There are lots of new features with the new look, and over the coming weeks we will be making more tweaks and changes.

Feel free to let us know what you think of the new look below.
