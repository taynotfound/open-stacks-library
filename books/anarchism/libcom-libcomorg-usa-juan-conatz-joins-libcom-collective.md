Juan and friends, celebrating. Libcom.org is very happy to announce that one of our US-based users, Juan Conatz, has joined the admin collective.

For a good few years, Juan has been the most prolific contributor to the libcom library , so we thought it was only fair that he should have a say in the running of the site. (And a share of the blame for unpopular admin decisions.)

He is a Minneapolis Jew Rican, warehouse worker, member of the revolutionary union the Industrial Workers of the World and one of the editors of the excellent Recomposition blog .

We are particularly pleased that he has joined and we hope that his involvement in the site will help libcom.org become less UK-centric, and more a global resource for English-speaking angry workers and libertarian communists. Also we are very happy that he will be able to help develop our Spanish-language content and start a revolutionary dubstep archive.

Assistance from our users would be appreciated in monitoring Juan's posts to ensure that from now on he spells it "organi s e".

As a joining bonus he will be allowed to ban one user of his choice, so be nice to him!
