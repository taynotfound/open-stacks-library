Fascism has come to power in the US. It now controls the largest nuclear arsenal in the world (a deeply ironic thought, considering how the official motivation for the original development of the atomic bomb in the Manhattan Project was ostensibly preventing such). It can unleash a massive propaganda machinery of automated production of disinformation through appropriately aligned AI generated text and images.
