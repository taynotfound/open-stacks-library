# Open Vistas: a bi-monthly of life and letters

Archive of Open Vistas , a 1925 magazine that published poetry and reviews, as well as essays reflecting the editors' anarchist politics.

## Open Vistas Vol. 1, No. 1 (January-February 1925)

The January-February 1925 issue of Open Vistas a magazine that published poetry and reviews, as well as essays reflecting the editors' anarchist politics.

CONTENTS

-Open vistas by the Editors

-Carved crosses by Rose Florence Freeman

-A school of the renaissance by Elisée Reclus

-Work of the artist and savant by Leonardo Da Vinci

-The nightingale by Bratescu-Voinesti

-Connoisseurs by Rose Florence Freeman

-For you mother by Ada Negri

-Oscar Wilde by D. Anghel

-Contrasts in miniature by Joseph Ishill

-Excerpts: E. Castelar, Havelock Ellis, William Godwin
