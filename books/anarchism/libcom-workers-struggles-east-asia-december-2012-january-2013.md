Workers in Wuhan, China protest over unpaid wages by dancing Gangnam Style Summary and links to news stories of workers' struggles around East Asia during December 2012 & January 2013 and related resources. The most important stories appear on my Twitter feed as soon as I find them: http://twitter.com/spartacusnews.

I was on holiday from halfway through December to halfway through January, so I've rolled both summaries into one and they're perhaps not as extensive as usual. I have also given up adding news from JTTP.cn and Jasmine Revolution for the moment, so please check them yourself (with the help of Google Translate or something similar if your Mandarin isn't up to scratch) for more news of strikes etc. in China.

News from other countries that I find whilst compiling these summaries appears on my website here though I'm a little behind at the moment. For those who are interested in helping or producing something similar to these summaries for other regions of the world, you may like to know that I am currently working on a complete rewrite of that website to allow both geeks and non-geeks to participate (the current version is horrible and unwieldy for everyone).

These last two months there has been news from Cambodia, China, Indonesia, Japan, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, North Korea, Philippines, Singapore, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, and Vietnam.
