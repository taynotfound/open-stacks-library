Three printable leaflet designs, with artwork by Marjane Satrapi and Crimethinc and texts by the Alliance of Middle Eastern and North African Socialists, Anarchist Communist Group and Crimethinc.

The designs for these leaflets were taken from graphics by Crimethinc .

The texts are based on Oppose U.S. and Iran War by Showing Solidarity with Uprisings in the MENA Region by the Alliance of Middle Eastern and North African Socialists , Neither Washington nor Tehran by the Anarchist Communist Group , and Against All Wars, Against All Governments: Understanding the US-Iran War by Crimethinc.

Also crimethinc has made Spanish translations of both images and their statement.
