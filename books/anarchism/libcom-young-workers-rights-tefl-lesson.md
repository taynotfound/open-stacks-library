A TEFL handout for advanced / upper-intermediate students from the book 'Functional skills English: health & social care workbook' (aimed at students who would like to find work in health care).

A reading on young workers' rights is followed by a series of comprehension exercises and a final written task. The text and some of the exercises could be used to teach should/shouldn't to give advice and in general the use of modal verbs. However, the figures are out-of-date as the original text is from 2012.

Good stuff. We made a Spanish lesson once about work conflicts of the CNT for language teachers here. It was well received.
