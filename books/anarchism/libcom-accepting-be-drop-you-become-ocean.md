Yellow submarines collective The "Yellow submarines collective" made this moving video based on an open letter that was signed by 1,400 artists and technicians titled “We will not be deceived", published in the newspaper Libération” on 5th May, 2019. https://en.labournet.tv/accepting-be-drop-you-become-ocean

In the letter they call on support for those that have been struggling as part of the Yellow Vests movement for the last six months. They oppose the negative reporting on the Yellow Vests from the mainstream media, and highlight that the violence that threatens us the most is economic and social violence.
