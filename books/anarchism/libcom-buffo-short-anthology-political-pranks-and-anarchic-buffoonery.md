A short pamphlet by Larry Law collecting instances of "revolutionary buffoonery." Digitised by Glasgow's Spirit of Revolt archive .

"Revolutionary buffoonery tries to build confidence, not cynicism; it tries to demystify, not alienate. But most of all it brings play back into everyday life.

It has always been the aim of jesters, by playing the Fool themselves, to expose the real fools."
