In English Online archive of Faridabad Majdoor Samachar, a workers´ newspaper in Hindi published and distributed since late 1980´s in and around the industrial region of Delhi. This region includes Faridabad, Udyog Vihar, Gurgaon, Delhi and Manesar. In the first week of every month around 9,000 copies of Samachar are distributed and shared mainly by the industrial workers. The newspaper sees itself as one of the media of constructing-finding ways of exchanging experiences and ideas of workers : for the way out.
