To Comrades and Friends: It is my intention to go East on an agitation trip in a week or two. I will deliver lectures upon the following subjects: “Curse of Child Labor,” “The Commune,” “Anarchism Defined,” “The Industrial Workers of the World: Their Aims and Object.” Comrades will please arrange meetings and communicate with me as soon as possible.
