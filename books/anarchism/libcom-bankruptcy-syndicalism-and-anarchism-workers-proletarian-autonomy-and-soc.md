Well having read it I'd say its more a critique of the CNT in the 70s that has been stretched to try and cover anarchism and syndicalism. A lot of the criticism of the CNT during this period are useful, but trying to sell this pamphlet as a wider critique seems incredibly dishonest, and undermines the opening assertions.
