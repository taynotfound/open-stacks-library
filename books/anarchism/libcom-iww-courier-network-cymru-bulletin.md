A bulletin produced by UberEats and Deliveroo workers with the IWW Couriers Network Cymru . First hosted on Notes From Below .

The first issue of this bulletin, produced by UberEats and Deliveroo workers with the IWW Couriers Network Cymru , focuses on the growth and first victories of the network.

It’s not the first workers’ bulletin produced in the sector - the Rebel Roo was a bulletin published for a year between the winters of 2016 and 2017.

The main action taken by the network so far has been against wait times at McDonalds. Workers from the network also released a statement in solidarity with the McStrike , showing that there is potential to link up these fights industrially.
