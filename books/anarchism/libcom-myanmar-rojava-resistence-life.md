This is the statement from a Myanmar resistence group named "Anti-Fascist Internationalist Front" to Rojava.

Contact Details for Anti-Fascist Internationalist Front are as follow: Anti-Fascist Internationalist Front 26-10-2024 [email protected]

On the 23rd of October, the fascist Turkish state launched a brutal attack against the people of Rojava and North-East Syria, as well as Sengal and the mountains of Southern Kurdistan. The targets of this attack were bakeries, hospitals, petrol and electricity stations, administration buildings, and among others, the fundamental infrastructure of the revolution. Only days before, from October 11 to October 20, the fascist State Administration Council of Myanmar led a brutal campaign of slaughter against the people of Budalin township, murdering at least 25 civillians, dismembering them, and displaying their remains in the villages. All the while, the indiscriminate bombing of civilians continues across the country. The objective of the fascist enemy is clear. Whether in the mountains of Kurdistan or in the mountains of Myanmar, the mercenaries are in every instance outmatched by the forces of revolution. Unable to secure victory on the battlefield, they instead bring terror and atrocities upon civilians who support the democratic forces. We reject the intimidation, indignity, and terror of fascism wherever it may arise, and stand in solidarity with the people of Rojava and the forces defending the revolution. As internationalists fighting alongside the peoples of Myanmar against the SAC junta, we take up the motif of our comrades fighting for a democratic modernity: Berxwedan Jiyan e, Resistance is life!
