Krasnaia nov was a soviet literary journal founded in 1921 by Aleksandr Voronsky

All material is from SovietLiterature , a site which makes available the texts and bibliographies of 1920s Soviet journals. Besides 'Red Virgin Soil', the site has texts from journals such as 'Left', 'Forge', 'At the post' and 'New left'. All texts are in Russian, so it is necessary to copy/paste the link of a text in to an online translator . That's also how the titles were translated, so apologies for any nonsense you'll find.

For the first couple of issues every title is linked to the text. After that, to save myself the trouble, only the text to a few titles of an issue is given; every following title has a link with a higher number. So for instance the first title of issue no.3 of 1921 is at http://www.ruthenia.ru/sovlit/j/86.html , the next title is at http://www.ruthenia.ru/sovlit/j/87.html , etc. Oh and another thing; some titles are not listed here, mostly because of sloppinness on my part, but they are there, somewhere.
