Last week, students in Croatia organized actions to express solidarity with their colleagues in UK and Italy who are protesting against tuition fee hikes and education cuts. In several Croatian cities, the students placed banners on prominent public and academic sites to alert the public of the continuing student struggle against the growing commercialization of education.
