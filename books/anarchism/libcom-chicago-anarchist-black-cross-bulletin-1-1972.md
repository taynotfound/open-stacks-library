With thanks to Kate Sharpley Library for providing a copy to be scanned.

It appears that at least seven issues of this bulletin were published between July 1972 and January 1974. Please leave a comment if you are able to help with including other editions on Libcom.

NB: This copy (and possibly all copies?) was not printed especially well, so a couple of pages are difficult to read. Page 4 was so illegible that it was reprinted as an insert between pages 12 and 13.
