Complete online archive of World Labour News , the English paper of the International Working Mens Association (i.e. the IWA, the syndicalist international). Eighteen issues were published bi-monthly from 1960-1962.

From January 1963, World Labour News was incorporated into the Syndicalist Workers Federation paper Direct Action .

PDFs courtesy of Sparrows Nest Archive, Splits and Fusions Archive and the personal collection of a Libcom contributor.

Splits and Fusions has more of these here: https://www.dropbox.com/sh/2se22gkc6ylerei/AAAmJCHe-V1xWuiqPdBvZhDaa/Syndicalist%20Workers%20Federation?dl=0&subfolder_nav_tracking=1

This is now a complete archive, so that's a nice way to round off the week.

An important piece of english speaking anarcho-syndicalism. Influential in my own development decades ago. Glad to see that Fossie threaded the needle and finished the weaving of our history and these publications.
