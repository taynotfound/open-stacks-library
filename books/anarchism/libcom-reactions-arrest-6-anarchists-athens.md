Journalist Union occupied by anarchists in Athens: "zero tolerance to state repression" Journalist Union headquarters in Athens occupied by anarchists, as "police evidence" are proved to be utter lies.

On Tuesday noon 200 anarchists moved into the high-rise headquarters of the Journalists' Union in down-town Athens and after allowing all workers to exit the building declared it occupied, dropping a banner on its front in solidarity to the 6 arrested anarchists accused by the police as members of the urban guerrilla group Revolutionary Struggle. The anarchist held the building for several hours before forming a demo and marching to Exarcheia.

Police allegations against the arrested, which are being fed to the population through the media in the form of huge terror-spectacle, are between the ridiculous and the grotesque with "evidence" consisting of little more than empty suitcases, carnival clothes, bamboo grove fences and hand-written notes on guerrilla communiques readily available at every bourgeois media website.

As an example of the media strategy meant to soil the name of anarchy in greece, last night’s main news all featured the story of the police having found mobile phone conversation between two of the arrested where one is watching the 15 year old Afghan boy blown up by a bomb last month and describing the scene to the other laughing. Of course, as the state TV has finally accepted this afternoon these “evidence” have been completely fabricated and bear no resemblance to reality. Already before the revelation of the evidence sham some of the bourgeois media are casting serious doubts on all of the evidence provided by the police as well as criticism TV stations as reproducing lies. For example the daily Eleftherotypia has pointed out that the rusty bullets allegedly shot in Mt Imitus “by one of the arrested while he was being watched by the chief of the anti-terrorist bureau days before their arrest”, are first of all very old (and thus rusty…) and in fact hunting-gun bullets and not AK ones.

Update: Bourgeois media claim that the false story involving the supposed phonecall at spot of the bomb that killed the 15 year old Afghan boy in March was directly orchestrated by the anti-terrorist bureau.

Police leaks, total or by choice, estimations and expectations of the Anti-terrorist bureau, references to ideas and to the action of the arrested (all of them very well known to the wider movement) are mingled with unbelievable reportage and an unbearable televised discourse-diarrhea creating a climate of incrimination and devaluation of the accused without any evidence. On the contrary, they lift to the status of 'evidence' facts like for example that [name of an accused] is the No.1 "usual suspect" for the lat decade and is being constantly watched, or that [name of another accused] was a friend of the (under unclear conditions killed by the police) Lambros Foundas, trying to convince us that being persecuted by the police or one's comradely relations are evidence of guilt. Thus they formulate a 'virtual reality' which presents as an unquestionable reality the police allegations and the screams of the TV-persecutors. Because many 'police truths' have been proved operations of cold interest, often by means of engrossing or constructing evidence, because we are at a period of a climax and generalisation of state repression (police brutality against immigrants and the youth, incontrolable phone surveillances, televised house invasions, 'operations virtue' like the one in the Navarinou park on the 12th of April, are hust a few examples), because the government and the political personnel of the state have every reason to obscure their anti-popular attack against the rights of the workers and the plebean layers of the population through a show of "security" (whose security we wonder), because the TV when it does not corrupt, it simply lies (who is really worse, the ridiculous puppets commenting on the latest porn scandal or the ridiculous ones who discover...bamboo groves in the homes of 'terrorists'?), because in Greece being an anarchist or sympathising with the armed action is not a penal offense (yet), and even less "a terrorist one", because, finally, especially in periods like the one we experience, we must be especially suspicious against the state and the archons, we advise soberness in judging and in evaluating, determination against state arbitrariness, and solidarity to whoever is a victim of it, for whatever reason that might be so.
