I write this as i find the Correctness in the communization theory, I write this to help bring communization theory to the proletariat. We shall prevail comrades!

In order for the proletariat to liberate itself, it must bring the revolutionary appropriation of the United productive forces, it must bring the revolutionary abolition of the state, of the division of labor, of exchange, of value, of property and all its forms, of the labor process, ect, ect, it must abolish capitalism and its relations and corresponding social relations of production, abolish its social order. For the proletariat to bring this revolutionary abolition, this communization to the forefront it must be revolutionary, but the proletariat can only be revolutionary by recognizing itself as a class, it can only be revolutionary once the proletariat sees that it is nothing but a category of the capitalist mode of production, once the proletariat sees it is nothing but a slave created for the self-valorization of capital i.e surplus-value. If the proletariat does not recognize itself as a class as a negative result of the capitalist mode of production, and therefore does not seek to abolish the very foundations that created it i.e capitalist relations of production, it will reproduce itself as a class, reproducing the value form and laboring the same labor process that keeps it a slave to capitalism. Only through the process of communization of society will the proletariat find its emancipation. For the proletariat to thus bring itself liberation the very content of the revolution must be communism, communization is the revolution itself.
