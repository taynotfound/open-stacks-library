# CIA FOIA Reading Room - Project STARGATE

**Source:** CIA FOIA Reading Room

Declassified documents from the CIA's 20-year remote viewing and psychic intelligence program, codenamed STARGATE. The collection covers human experimentation, classified protocols, and government funding of paranormal research from the 1970s through 1995. Released in full after program closure under FOIA mandate.
