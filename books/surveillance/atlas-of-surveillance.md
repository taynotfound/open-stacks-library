# Atlas of Surveillance

**Source:** Electronic Frontier Foundation  

A searchable database mapping which US police departments use which surveillance tech  -  ALPRs, face recognition, drones, Ring/Flock camera networks, predictive policing. Crowd-sourced, city by city. See what's deployed where you live.
