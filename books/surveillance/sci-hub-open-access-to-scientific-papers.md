# Sci-Hub - Open Access to Scientific Papers

**Source:** Sci-Hub

Sci-Hub provides free, direct access to tens of millions of scientific papers and journal articles, bypassing publisher paywalls. For surveillance and censorship researchers it unlocks peer-reviewed literature on surveillance architecture, signals intelligence, and internet censorship that would otherwise cost hundreds of dollars per paper. Legally contested globally, with multiple injunctions against it, but it continues to operate across rotating domains.
