# The Torture Archive - Post-9/11 Detention & Interrogation

**Source:** National Security Archive (GWU)

A comprehensive collection of declassified records, government memos, and FOIA releases documenting U.S. torture and rendition policies after September 11. Includes the Torture Memos from the OLC, ICRC reports on CIA black sites, and Defense Department interrogation authorizations. An essential primary-source record of legally sanctioned abuse.
