# NSA SIDtoday Newsletters (Snowden Archive)

**Source:** The Intercept

Full searchable archive of SIDtoday, the NSA Signals Intelligence Directorate's internal newsletter published from 2003 onward, leaked by Edward Snowden and published by The Intercept. Over 500 articles reveal day-to-day NSA operations, surveillance targets, and analyst culture. Provides an unprecedented insider view of mass surveillance programs.
