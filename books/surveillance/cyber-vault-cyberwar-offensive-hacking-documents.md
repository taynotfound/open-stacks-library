# Cyber Vault - Cyberwar & Offensive Hacking Documents

**Source:** National Security Archive (GWU)

Curated collection of declassified and leaked government documents on U.S. offensive cyber operations, NSA hacking programs, and interagency cybersecurity policy. Covers Stuxnet, Presidential Policy Directives on cyberwar, and classified NSA exploit frameworks. Draws from FOIA releases, congressional records, and the Snowden disclosures.
