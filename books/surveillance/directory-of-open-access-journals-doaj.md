# Directory of Open Access Journals (DOAJ)

**Source:** DOAJ

DOAJ indexes over 20,000 fully open-access, peer-reviewed academic journals covering political science, media studies, internet governance, digital surveillance, and civil liberties. All indexed journals are vetted for editorial standards and are freely accessible without paywalls. An authoritative starting point for scholarly literature on censorship regimes and surveillance infrastructure.
