# Power of Women journal

Online archive of the journal of the London-based Power of Women collective. Published in the mid 1970s.

At least five issues were published from 1974-1976.

## Power of Women #1 (1974)

Debut issue of the journal of the Power of Women Collective, including articles on women in Northern Ireland, shoplifting, social security, sexual politics, etc.

## Contents

- Editorial - The Power of Women Collective

- Northern Ireland: A Woman's Place - Some women from The Power of Women Collective

- Interview with a shoplifter

- When money comes from the state and not the man (social security / wages for housework)

- Collectively Speaking (recent discussions of the collective: prices, boycotts, price controls)

- What is a Sex Object?

- The Invisible Woman (autobiographical piece on growing up gay, feminism, work etc)- Evelyn Farrar

- Letters

- International Feminist Collective statement

- Salt of the earth in Harlan County (Kentucky miners strike)

## Power of Women #2 (1974)

Including: women in Northern Ireland, Family Allowance campaign, wages for housework, wages for school work, nursing, Imperial Typewriters dispute, etc.

## Contents

- Editorial - Power of Women collective

- Living through the crisis: women in Northern Ireland and Britain - Rose Craig

- The end... Family Allowance - Priscilla Allen

- The beginning... Wages for Housework

- Wages for School Work - interview with two students from a school in South London

- This is nursing - Lizzie Stuart

- Background to nursing struggles - Priscilla Allen

- Agency nurses

- Nurses march in London and Bristol

- Welcome to Britain (nurses training) - Bernadette

- Imperial(ist) Typewriters strike - Mala Dhondy

- Women against women: another form of racism - Beese

## Power of Women #5 (1976)

Including: family allowance freeze in Canada, welfare campaigns in US, Black Women for wages for housework launched, women's general strike in Iceland, international Lesbian conference, women factory workers in Italy, sex work in France, etc.

- Baby's due (song)

- 'The state is the biggest pimp' (sex workers strike in France)

- 'All Work and No Pay' - viewers respond to wages for housework TV programme

Possibly the last issue of Power of Women. The masthead changed to "magazine of the international wages for housework campaign" for this issue.
