# Gay Left: A Socialist Journal Produced by Gay Men


A complete online archive of Gay Left, published in the UK from 1975-1980. Alongside useful content on gay politics from this period, the journal includes reactionary material advocating for paedophiles. We do not endorse this and include it for reference purposes only - as a record of the mistakes made in a previous era.
