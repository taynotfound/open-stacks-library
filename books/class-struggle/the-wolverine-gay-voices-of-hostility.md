# The Wolverine - Gay Voices of Hostility


Online archive of The Wolverine, a class struggle anarcho-gay newsletter loosely connected to the London Autonomists/Monday Group - some members of whom were also involved in the early incarnation of Class War. Published in London in the early 1980s. It is unclear how many issues were produced. Possibly three? If you know more - leave a comment...
