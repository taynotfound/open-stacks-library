# Wages For Housework Notebooks

Anthologies of texts compiled by feminist collectives in Montreal and New York in the 1970s.

## Wages For Housework Notebooks #1 (1975)

An anthology of texts compiled by Montreal Power of Women Collective.

## Contents

- Introduction - Montreal Power of Women Collective

- Wages for Housework - Guiliana Pompei

- The Perspective of Wages for Housework - Power of Women Collective (London)

- When Wages for Housework becomes a Perspective - Silvia Federici

With thanks to Arlen Austin for making the scan available.

## Wages For Housework Notebooks #2 (1975)

An anthology of texts by members of the Power of Women Collective (London) compiled by the Wages for Housework Collective (New York).

## Contents

- Introduction - Nicole Cox

- Speech at International House - Selma James

- When the mute speaks: The work of creating a movement - Selma James

- In defence of feminism - A London conference report - Priscilla Allen and Sylvine Schmidt

With thanks to Arlen Austin for making the scan available.
