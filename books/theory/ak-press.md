# AK Press

**Source:** AK Press

One of the world's largest anarchist and radical publishing houses, operating as a worker-owned cooperative since 1990. AK Press publishes and distributes books, zines, and pamphlets spanning anarchist theory, labor history, anti-fascism, and radical feminism. Their catalog is an essential reference for serious study of anarchist and leftist thought.
