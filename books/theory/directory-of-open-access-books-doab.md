# Directory of Open Access Books (DOAB)

**Source:** Directory of Open Access Books

DOAB indexes over 80,000 peer-reviewed open-access academic books from hundreds of publishers, including significant holdings in political science, surveillance studies, and human rights law. All listed titles are fully open access and legally free to download. A critical resource for finding rigorously peer-reviewed, citable scholarship without paywalls.
