# Library Genesis

**Source:** Library Genesis (libgen.li)

Library Genesis is one of the oldest and largest shadow-library repositories, hosting millions of books and academic articles spanning political theory, surveillance studies, and censorship history. It has historically served as critical reading infrastructure for researchers and activists in countries with restricted library access. Legally grey in most Western jurisdictions; presented here as contested but functioning knowledge infrastructure.
