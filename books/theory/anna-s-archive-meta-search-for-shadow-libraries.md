# Anna's Archive - Meta Search for Shadow Libraries

**Source:** Anna's Archive

Anna's Archive is the largest open-source meta-search engine indexing shadow libraries including LibGen, Sci-Hub and others, covering tens of millions of books, papers, and documents. It is an access-to-knowledge tool for researchers in regions where academic paywalls or state censorship block access to political science, surveillance studies, and human rights literature. Its legal status is contested; it is listed here as a pointer to knowledge infrastructure, not an endorsement of infringement.
