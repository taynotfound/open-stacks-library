# PM Press

**Source:** PM Press

A radical independent publisher and distributor founded by veterans of the left publishing world, focusing on labor, class struggle, anarchism, feminism, and global resistance movements. PM Press produces books, pamphlets, zines, and audiovisual media with an explicit commitment to social justice and anti-capitalist politics. Their catalog is broad and internationally oriented.
