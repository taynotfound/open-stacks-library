# Recipes for Disaster: An Anarchist Cookbook

**Source:** CrimethInc.

A practical anarchist organizing handbook covering consensus decision-making, security culture, affinity groups, and a wide range of collective-action skills. Despite the subtitle it is CrimethInc.'s movement-education manual, not a weapons guide. It remains a foundational, non-dogmatic resource for organizers across many movement contexts.
