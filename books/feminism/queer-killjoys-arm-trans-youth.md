# Arm Trans Youth

*by Queer Killjoys*

SEIZE THE MEANS OF TRANSITION!
  

DON’T WAIT FOR PERMISSION!

“PROTECTION”? Who is protecting who? And from what?

Who made cis parents the spokespeople for trans children’s liberation? Is this about the autonomy of children or is it really about “PARENTAL RIGHTS” of ownership?

Why should we beg the “australian” state to care for trans kids’ health when “australia” protects white parents and REMOVES BLAK CHILDREN? LOCKS UP BLAK CHILDREN IN YOUTH DETENTION AT THE HIGHEST RATES OR PLACES THEM IN RACIST FOSTER CARE SYSTEMS UNDER THE GUISE OF “PROTECTION”? It locks children up at its borders and supplies weapons used to kill children around the globe.

white families CAN demand the state’s protection for their WHITE children and this betrays the true purpose behind the call to “protect trans youth”: the ‘aussie’ entity does not protect or care for children but merely protects its own white supremacist reproduction by USING CHILDREN AS AN INSTRUMENT.

DOCTORS AND PSYCHIATRISTS ARE NOT THE SOLUTION TO TRANS “PROBLEMS”—

THEY ARE IN THE BUSINESS OF GENOCIDE, OF SELECTION AND EXTERMINATION, OF FORCED STERILIZATION, OF DISPOSSESSION, OF DETENTION AND BRAINWASHING AND TORTURE.

Yet parents and LGBT NGOs appeal to the same state forces that have abused and sterilized and mutilated queer and trans people for centuries.

The reality is that ‘protection’ can only mean ASSIMILATION. And assimilation equals death.

Refuse the trap of “safety” and “family friendly,” used for decades to justify violence against dangerous queers like us, supposedly preying on your straight-and-narrow kids. Talk of “safety” is infantalising deradicalising AND DANGEROUS.

Refuse the implied rights of parents over their children, a logic TERFs appeal to when destroying kids lives.

NGO mummies and daddies of the movement will tell you that we can’t do anything to jeopardise the “safety” of trans children…but why won’t they let trans kids speak for themselves?

Take hormones illegally! Defy your parents! Defy “your” state!

Trans youth must be ARMED with the means to liberate themselves: armed with the knowledge of themselves and their history, armed with the tools for self-defence in all its forms from analysis to action, armed with the means to transition collectively and autonomously with their peers, armed by a new non-hierarchical trans family, a community that teaches defiance.

Seek instead the reciprocal parenting that trans people offer each other when we give birth to ourselves, a liberatory kinship free from the hierarchies and violence of the nuclear parent-child relationship.

A radically transformed social relation is the only guarantee of gender affirmation.

Stop fighting to stay in the small group of people who benefit from living in this colony! Band together to SMASH this colony and take what we need!

A liberated land will free suppressed forms of care and life beyond the state and its colonial eugenicist gender binary.

Your parents will not protect you!
  

Your doctors will not protect you!
  

NGOs will not protect you!
  

“australia” will not protect you!

ARM TRANS YOUTH
