# Zerowork journal

Comprehensive online archive of the Zerowork journal, an autonomist Marxist-influenced magazine based in the US which published two issues between 1975 and 1977.

Some articles retrieved from the excellent http://zerowork.org/ - including contents of the unpublished third issue.

See also Zerowork: the background for extensive commentary, background documents etc.
