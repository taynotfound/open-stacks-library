# Communist Interventions series of readers


A series of readers by the Communist Research Cluster from Oakland, California and Brooklyn, New York, founded in 2014 publishing abridged readers on various aspects of communism and class struggle. There are now three volumes in the Communist Interventions series. These cover the European socialist and communist traditions, the US Black revolutionary tradition, and the revolutionary feminisms of the West.
