# The Labour Debate: An Investigation into the Theory and Reality of Capitalist Work - Ana C. Dinerstein & Michael Neary

The book featuring contributions by John Holloway, Simon Clarke, Werner Bonefeld, Harry Cleaver, Ana C. Dinerstein, Massimo De Angelis, Michael Neary, Glenn Rikowski, Graham Taylor.
