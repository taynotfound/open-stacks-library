# Angry Workers' Bulletin


Online archive of Angry Workers Bulletin, published in the mid to late 1980s in Berkeley, California. The publication's influences included the German and Dutch left-communists and the Situationist International. Not to be confused with Angry Workers of the World. The scans of issues 1 and 2 are courtesy of the Splits and Fusions archive.
