# Solidarity for workers' full control - Solidarity (West London)

Partial online archive of the journal of the West London group of libertarian socialist organisation, Solidarity, from the late 1960s/early 1970s.

## Solidarity for workers' full control #01

First issue of the West London Solidarity group's journal, probably from 1969 or 1970, with articles about the Liverpool GEC occupation attempt, cutlery workers in Sheffield, refuse collectors in Brent and minicab drivers.
