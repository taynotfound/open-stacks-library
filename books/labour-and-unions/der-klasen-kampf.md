# Der Klasen Kampf

Partial online archive of English translations of the Yiddish language IWW newspaper, Der Klasen Kampf, published around 1919 in the US.

## Der Klasen Kampf 1.02

English translation of issue 2 from volume 1 of Yiddish language IWW newspaper, Der Klasen Kampf, published in the US in 1919.

Thanks to Kenyon Zimmer for finding and digitising the Yiddish language original . Kenyon found it in a Bureau of Immigration file of radical materials that had been sent to IWW members being detained for deportation on Ellis Island during the First Red Scare, and may be the only surviving copy.
