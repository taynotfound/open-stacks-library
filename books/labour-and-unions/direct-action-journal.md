# Direct Action journal

Direct Action was published by the Industrial Workers of the World Australian Administration.

It printed 135 issues between January 1914 and August 1917. Twelve more issues were published between May 1928 and May 1929 and another two in November 1930. A history of the paper can be found here.

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 01

Covering January to August 1914.

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 02

From September 1914 to the beginning of 1915.

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 03

From mid January 1915 to June 1915, includes an expanded May Day Issue (No.28).

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 04

From mid June to October 1915, includes the first weekly issue of Direct Action (No.40).

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 05

Covers October 2925 to December 1915.

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 06

Covers the 25th December 1915 to the beginning of March 1916.

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 07

Covers events that took place between March and May 1916.

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 08

Covers events during May to July 1916.

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 09

Covers events from July to September 1916. Also includes a special edition about mining (No.88).

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 10

Covers October to December 1916. Issues 91, 94 and 96 feature new cover designs, and No. 92 marks Direct Actions peak of over 8,000 paying readers.

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 11

Covers December 1916 to February 1917.

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 12

Covers March to May 1917. Includes a special Mayday issue (No.120)

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 13

Covers May to the middle of July 1917. Also includes a special frame up issue (No.126).

## Direct Action Journal: Batch 14

Covers July to August 1917. Issue No.131 includes supplementary material and No.135 is a special expanded issue.
