# Views & Comments

Views & Comments is a publication of the Metro Philadelphia Chapter of the Workers’ Solidarity Alliance , an anarchist-syndicalist activist group with members from all over North America. WSA has Friends status with the International Workers’ Association (IWA-AIT).

## Views & Comments #1

Contains articles on the Wet’suwet’en Nation, Roe v Wade, police funding and the imprisonment of Kirill Ukraintsev.

## Views & Comments #2

The August 2022 issue contains articles on reproductive activism, child labor, anarchist education and the Labor Notes conference.
