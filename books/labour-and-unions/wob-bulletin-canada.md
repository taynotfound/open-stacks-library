## Wob Bulletin (Canada) - #1

The following is a bulletin put together by a couple of branches of the IWW in Canada, originally hosted at Organizing Work .

While the outside support mustered for Canada Post workers has been both inspiring and effective , these IWW members’ position is that worker power on the shop floor remains the most important part of the puzzle. Here, they suggest a form of shopfloor action that can actually take advantage of the current contract.

The same type of action can be carried out at any workplace, which makes this piece of broader relevance.
