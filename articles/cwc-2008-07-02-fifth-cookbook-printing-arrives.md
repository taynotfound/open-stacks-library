We received the fifth printing of our anarchist cookbook [_Recipes for Disaster_](https://crimethinc.com/books/recipes-for-disaster) yesterday, and after unloading and moving 9,000 pounds of them in 90º heat, we can now say that these books are imbued with our sweat in more than just a figurative sense. Only very minor changes were made in this printing: the currency rates in the conversion table were updated and the comic book font used on two pages was changed for a third time (we think we got it right this time, _really_).
