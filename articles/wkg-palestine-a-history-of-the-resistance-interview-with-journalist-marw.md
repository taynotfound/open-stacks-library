[Marwa Osman is a mother of three. She holds PhD in Management on the &#8220;Effect of Intergenerational Differences on Workplace Productivity in Lebanon&#8221; from the Doctoral School of the Lebanese University, and two master’s degree on &#8220;The Effect of politics on the Foreign Direct Investment in Lebanon&#8221; from the Lebanese University, and on &#8220;Applying Knowledge Management in a Media Institution&#8221; from the Lebanese University. She is also a member of the Blue Peace Media Network for trans-boundary water management in the Middle East. Osman is a University Lecturer at the Lebanese International University and Maaref University. She hosts and produces the political show The MidEaStream. She is a writer and her comments on issues of West Asia on several international and regional media outlets are published by various outlets.]
