The Trump administration has accelerated its attacks on leftist organizations and individuals while also justifying its genocidal siege of Cuba with the release of the document "Cuba: the Capital of 21st Century Communism." This follows Executive Order National Security Presidential Memorandum-7 (NSPM-7), which provides a rationale for state repression and the criminalization of left organizing. Ajamu Baraka, Black Agenda Report contributing editor and Director of the North-South Project for Peoples-Centered Human Rights. He provides analysis on this newest iteration of state repression in the US.
