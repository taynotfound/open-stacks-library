On Thursday, both [Donald Trump](https://www.cnsnews.com/article/washington/susan-jones/trump-promises-announcement-next-week-democrat-run-cities-were-going) and his [white nationalist](https://itsgoingdown.org/what-you-need-to-know-about-stephen-millers-connection-to-richard-spencer-white-nationalism-why-it-matters/) and [nativist](https://crimethinc.com/2017/07/03/nativism-one-of-the-foundations-of-us-xenophobia-an-old-doctrine-of-bigotry-and-hatred-reemerges-today) advisor [Stephen Miller](https://twitter.com/MichaelEHayden/status/1283804925387046912) announced that they will begin deploying armed officers from the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) on the streets of cities that have seen large-scale protests—specifically, cities governed by Democrats. According to _[The Daily Mail](https://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-8526741/Donald-Trump-claims-announce-federal-action-week-against-cities.html),_ Trump stated he would "be looking at Seattle, Minneapolis, Portland, and Chicago." To understand what the stakes are, we focus on Portland, where DHS agents have already been brutalizing demonstrators for weeks.

# Intensifying Brutality

Starting in late May, Portland police mobilized riot troops daily to push protesters off the street, [targeting and brutalizing journalists](https://www.opb.org/news/article/aclu-sues-portland-oregon-police-officers-attacked-journalists-blm-protests/) and turning the downtown into a shooting gallery with tear gas and "less lethal munitions." DHS agents first joined them in brutalizing protesters weeks ago. According to the _[Williamette Weekly](https://www.wweek.com/news/2020/07/15/federal-officers-sent-by-president-trump-run-downtown-little-restrains-them/),_

> For the past two weeks, federal officers have patrolled the blocks surrounding the Mark O. Hatfield United States Courthouse. That's thanks mostly to President Donald Trump, who deployed the Department of Homeland Security to at least three U.S. cities that had seen significant street protests—Portland, Seattle, and Washington, DC.

[[ https://twitter.com/itsmaregine/status/1283862881633632257 ]]

In a [DHS memo](https://web.archive.org/web/20200716195852/https://www.dhs.gov/news/2020/07/16/acting-secretary-wolf-condemns-rampant-long-lasting-violence-portland) explaining the deployment to Portland, described by the [Portland Mercury](https://www.portlandmercury.com/blogtown/2020/07/16/28645782/dhs-director-decries-violent-anarchists-taking-over-portland) as "an inflammatory statement riddled with inaccuracies and spelling errors" and in some cases lacking even the most basic punctuation, acting DHS secretary Chad Wolf repeats the phrase "violent anarchists" 72 times, using this phrase to designate a total of several thousand people. In many cases, he brands a group of hundreds "violent anarchists" on account of the alleged actions of just a couple individuals. Of course, secretary Wolf and his cronies have no way of knowing the politics of every person involved in the protests. Rather, their goal is to carry out classic totalitarian propaganda: in repeating this phrase endlessly, they seek to invent an enemy to justify a militarized takeover.

As the _[Portland Mercury](https://www.portlandmercury.com/blogtown/2020/07/16/28645782/dhs-director-decries-violent-anarchists-taking-over-portland)_ pointed out,

> Wolf did not mention that the majority of violence Portlanders have witnessed over the past 47 days has come from the hands of the police.

While DHS officials publish fear-mongering announcements about "violent anarchists" painting messages on the plywood covering the windows of the Hatfield courthouse in Portland, federal agents have been set loose on demonstrators throughout the city. On July 11, federal agents in Portland [shot](https://www.opb.org/news/article/federal-officers-portland-protester-shot-less-lethal-munitions/) 26-year-old Donavan LaBella in the head with an impact munition, fracturing his skull. LaBella required facial reconstruction surgery and was still in serious condition at the hospital days later.

# The Two Faces of Fascism

For years, the Portland Police and the Department of Homeland Security [have worked with fascist and far-right organizers](https://twitter.com/IGD_News/status/1284009064653942784) to coordinate their demonstrations and facilitate their violence against anti-racist and anti-fascist counter-protesters as well as the general public. In June 2018, DHS worked directly with far-right leader Joey Gibson to plan a rally in downtown Portland during which fascists were permitted to attack counter-demonstrators with impunity. In early 2019, texts between Gibson and members of the Portland Police Bureau [came to light](https://www.muckrock.com/news/archives/2019/apr/24/pbb-patriot-prayer-logs/), revealing that the police were feeding Gibson information, letting him know when his colleagues that were on probation needed to lay low, and informed him in advance about anti-fascist events and activities. Police [faced no consequences](https://www.portlandmercury.com/blogtown/2019/09/12/27136199/police-officer-who-sent-protective-texts-to-joey-gibson-cleared-of-wrongdoing-in-city-review) for this.

# No One Is Coming to Save Us

For now, Trump and his supporters are making great efforts to justify the intervention of federal officers in Portland, to such an extent that the governor of Oregon has accused him of simply attempting to pull "[an election stunt](https://www.wweek.com/news/2020/07/16/oregon-gov-kate-brown-says-president-trump-is-invading-portland-as-an-election-stunt/)." But if this becomes normalized, one day, the Department of Homeland Security and other federal agencies will intervene all around the country on a regular basis, without need of justification—attacking and permanently injuring protesters, kidnapping activists in unmarked cars, and suppressing protest by lethal force if necessary. Trump is not just a rogue demagogue; he is testing the balance of power on behalf of the entire ruling class, trying to figure out just how much they can get away with.

The brutality of the DHS and the Portland police illustrates why people [rebelled](https://crimethinc.com/2020/06/17/snapshots-from-the-uprising-accounts-from-three-weeks-of-countrywide-revolt) in response to the murder of the George Floyd in the first place. There is no question of the government or the police being accountable to us. The only way we can gain leverage on them is by becoming [ungovernable](https://crimethinc.com/2015/05/05/feature-why-we-dont-make-demands), making it impossible for them to keep their economy running at our expense. The answer is not to petition the authorities for more stringent rules governing state and federal intervention—a [doomed venture](https://twitter.com/crimethinc/status/1281631755305398273)—but to delegitimize all the forces of oppression, from the federal to the local level, and organize together to make it impossible for them to rule us.

The work of liberation that they began still waits for us to finish it today.
