From Act for Freedom Now!

My Dearest Ones

From the Nowhere I hold you close to my heart and want you to know that, for the most part, I’m doing well! Unfortunately, the last few days have been a bit tough, as this anniversary kept catapulting me back to that day when I had to leave you all behind. Buried feelings resurfaced as a result of my vivid visualization of those times. It’s been 10 years… And even though time is an illusion, I keep falling back into believing in it. But now, as I write these lines to you, the past is peeling away from the old facade, and a gust of wind sweeps the future away. I am with you, whether Here or There. And there is nothing that can sever this connection.

Ten years ago, I made the decision to set out on a journey to preserve my freedom. And this freedom allowed me to explore life in all its social and cultural diversity, thereby gaining a better understanding of myself and growing from it. An endeavor that is well worth the price I’ve paid!

I can’t even express how much I’m looking forward to seeing you all again. It’s going to be wonderful. Until then, let’s stay apart, yet remain close in spirit.

I love and miss you all.

Your friend and comrade from Nowhere
July 10, 2026
