We've introduced a new option for printing our zines in case you need to go easy on the toner.

When you’re printing zines, do you pay for toner? [We certainly didn’t, back in the day](/2011/04/15/2nd-annual-steal-something-from-work-day#appendix-i). But times change, and means of photocopying come and go. The one thing that doesn’t change is the [necessity](https://www.sproutdistro.com/catalog/zines/organizing/distroist-society/) to mass-produce and distribute zines by the thousand.

It has come to our attention that some of our zine designs burn through toner at an alarming pace. This could be an obstacle for those who are paying for toner themselves. What possesses a designer to fill a zine layout with vast swathes of pitch black? Probably, among other things, never having to be the one responsible for acquiring toner cartridges. Nice work if you can get it!

Fortunately, as often happens, one industrious CrimethInc. agent had already solved this problem autonomously, preparing her own toner-lite versions of nearly three dozen of our zines.  It only remained for us to make her solution accessible to everyone else.

So we’ve added a new feature to the [zine library](/zines) on this site: **Ink Lite.** If you select this option when you are downloading a zine, it will take you to a version of the design that is modified to save ink.

Don’t sleep on our zine library! It includes 126 different titles—something for everyone. We’ve recently added the [zine version](/zines/direct-action-guide) of our classic [Civilian’s Guide to Direct Action](/2017/03/14/direct-action-guide), which has existed since 2017 but somehow never made its way into the collection until now. We've also added a [zine version](/zines/understanding-the-rico-charges) of our coverage of the [RICO charges](/2023/09/05/understanding-the-rico-charges-in-atlanta-a-sweeping-indictment-seeks-to-criminalize-protest-itself) in Atlanta.

**Ink Lite** is not to be confused with the [lite version of our site](https://lite.crimethinc.com), which you can use if you don't have a lot of internet bandwidth at your disposal and need to load this website without images. You can find the option to switch to the lite version at the very bottom of every page on the site, or just navigate directly to [lite.crimethinc.com](https://lite.crimethinc.com).
