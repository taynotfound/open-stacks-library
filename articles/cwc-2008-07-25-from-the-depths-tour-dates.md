Today anarcho-punk maniacs From the Depths are embarking on [their summer tour](http://www.fromthedepths.info/shows.html), taking with them tremendous quantities of books and free literature. They'll be presenting a range of direct action, self-defense, and health care workshops, and are traveling in a truck that runs on vegetable oil so as to be able to play as many benefit shows for local grassroots projects as possible. To reach them for further information about any of these shows, or to invite them to your town, email [booking@fromthedepths.info](mailto:booking@fromthedepths.info).

Lyrics, downloadable artwork, and tour updates are all available at [www.fromthedepths.info](http://www.fromthedepths.info).
