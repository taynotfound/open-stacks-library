Long-running abolitionist podcast and radio show&nbsp; Kite Line&nbsp; features an interview on resisting ICE raids in Los Angeles, CA.&nbsp; Listen and Download&nbsp; HERE This episode focuses on the ICE raids still accelerating across the country, and the resistance spreading in response. We speak with K, an organizer in Los Angeles, who reflects on the ICE invasion and the movement against it, which has become a beacon for struggles across the US. Since Trump&rsquo;s Big Beautiful Bill, Immigration and Customs Enforcement has grown into the largest police force in the country and it&rsquo;s clear that future movements against policing will have to place organizing against ICE, borders, and citizenship at their core.
