The 9th printing of the [Gender Poster](https://crimethinc.com/posters) was completed yesterday morning—which happens to be almost exactly the 5th anniversary of the [first printing of the Gender poster](https://crimethinc.com/2003/08/09/crimethinc-gender-subversion-kit-trouble-a-brewing-update-coming-soon). That's about 175,000 copies in 5 years, which we consider a pretty decent start, and worthy of celebrating with an over-the-top video.
