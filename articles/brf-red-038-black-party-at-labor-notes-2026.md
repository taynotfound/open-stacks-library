by Black Rose/Rosa Negra &#8211; Labor Committee We&#8217;re excited to announce the third installment of our Red & Black Party at the 2026 Labor Notes Conference in Chicago, IL. Every two years Labor Notes brings together thousands of unionists from around the US with the goal of creating a more robust and fighting labor movement. Members of Black Rose have been involved in Labor Notes as attendees, panelists, and volunteers over the last decade. Since 2018 we&#8217;ve hosted our independent Red & Black Party at the bi-annual gathering, aiming to reach out to worker attendees who share our principles and are committed to building a militant, class struggle, and rank-and-file driven approach to labor organizing. Our 2024 party hosted more than 600 guests throughout the night. This year we&#8217;re going even bigger. Come through! Where: Hyatt Regency O&#8217;Hare &#8211; LAX Room When: 9 PM &#8211; Late Cost: Free, but bring cash for the bar. Featuring: DJ sets by D-Rail and Tsibinki .
