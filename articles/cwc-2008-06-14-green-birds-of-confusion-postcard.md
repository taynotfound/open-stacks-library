We just printed a new version of the [birds of confusion](https://crimethinc.com/2007/09/26/letterpressed-postcard/) postcard on a nice Spring Green paper with Robin's Egg Blue and Metallic Silver inks. Naming colors is fun. We did 1600 copies on our letterpress—6400 impressions over a few days—and they are now going out free in [every paid order](http://store.crimethinc.com/x/), and should last at least 6 weeks. Thanks again to [Richard Olmsted](http://richardolmsted.com) for the artwork!
