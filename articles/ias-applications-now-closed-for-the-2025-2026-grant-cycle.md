Applications for the 2025-2026 Anarchist Horizons and Jen Angel Anarchist Media Grants are now closed. We received nearly 250 applications this year and have begun the review process. Applicants for this grant cycle will hear back on or before June 8th, 2026 about whether they have received the grant. We may also contact you with follow up questions to help our decision process between now and then. Make sure to keep an eye on the email you submitted the grant application with! That is where you will receive our decision, and we will need confirmation of your acceptance of the grant as quickly as possible, or we will need to move on to another grantee and those funds will no longer be available for your project. Thank you for all of your excellent grant applications and we are excited to fund as many of them as we are able.
