[**Listen here.**](https://crimethinc.com/podcast/no-wall-they-can-build-8) Welcome to **Episode 8** of [No Wall They Can Build](https://crimethinc.com/books/no-wall-they-can-build), the [Ex-Worker Podcast](http://crimethinc.com/podcast)’s serialized audiobook exploring borders and migration across North America. This installment continues to explore the reality of border policy by taking a closer look at the agency that enforces it, describing the atrocities that its agents perpetrate and their mindsets to justify it. The Border Patrol is merely one of the largest and most vicious players in a game that brings lucrative profits to a host of players on both sides of the border, at the expense of the vulnerable migrants who are driven into the desert. The episode concludes with a discussion of the environmental landscape of the desert itself and a poignant reflection on its harsh beauty, envisioning the healing of the land when one day it will no longer be scarred by borders.
