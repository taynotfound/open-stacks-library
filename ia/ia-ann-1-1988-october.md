THE FREE SCHOOL 
~“ The free school has been in existance since april 88, and 
came out of a friendship/ co parenting situation and the desire 
for our kids to at least begin with learning based on free 
expression and anti-authoritarian structures. At present there 
are 5 kids and about 8 adults on roster which isn't enough as 
we made a decision to have two adults on every rostered day. 
The school operates on Fridays 11-4, Saturdays 10-3 and Mondays 
10-3. On Fridays and Mondays two of them at least have been 
regularly going to kids club, an after school program in. Clif- 
ton Hill basically to get a feel of what it's like to be with 
bulk amount of kids and to cope with not having such individ 
ual attention - they love it. The times of the school are rel- 
atively flexible as we all know each other and so tend to org 
anize depending on who is around and whether we actually need 
to go to union street. We have our own room and the kids s&éem 
to like that sense of their own space to do as they wish with. 
There are excursions to nature reserves and local national 
parks for nature learning,cooking,creative plays, story making, 
camp fires in the back yard, reading, writing, songs,swimming, 
oplaying games, gardening,(front garden was done by the kids 
with some assistance-thanx Kat!] The more laborious activities 
such as sums, writing (letters), reading etc are done with 
lots of imagination to keep up the concentration, works well! 
We agreed to have a theme every three months, just for focus 
august was the body and september, the environment. If anyone 
shas skills or activities they enjoy and would like to share - 
with the kids, let us know. It is a truly evolutionary process 
and we try to have day long workshops to talk about ideas, 
difficulties, what seems to work etc. 
It is vitally important that people using the house are 
conscious that children use the building as well. We have H 
found at times that things were left in the back yard, for 
example, that coul;d be potentially dangerous to small persons 
So please BE AWARE. It would be good to have more money 
coming in so as we can build up resources: any donations 
welcome. Any adults wanting to be on our roster can come to 
one of our meetings, every second friday 4pm at union street 
and the next one is on the 30/9. We want people who are = 
committed and would be consistant, it is important for the 
children in terms of building up trust and important for 
activities to have a continuity. 
It seems to be a viable way forward out of the dangerous 
limitations schools of the state offer. Here's to happy, 
healthy kids in our future with NO RESTRICTION on their 
capacity to learn and grow. We hdpe to see more -localised 
community schools crop up around the place.... 


Petrine for the free school. 


_ANARCHO-SYNDICALIST FEDERATION 

WHERE WE STAND 

1.Qur aim is to create and defend a free and equal society. 

2.We are a revolutionary labour movement that uses as its only means of 
struggle,direct action in all its forms-occupations, strikes, boycott,sabotage, 
the general strike,etc.We are independent from all hierarchic and reformist 
unions and political parties,and we are actively creating an alternative to 
these and to existing society.We do not seek to gain politicalpower,but rather 
to see it distributed equally amongst all. 

3.We are a federation of autonomous anarcho-syndicalist groups practising 
co-operation and mutual aid.We have no leaders.All members have an equal part 
in the making of decisions.Responsibilities within the group are carried out 
by recallable delegates on a voluntary basis.Decisions of the federation are 
subject to agreement by the member groups. 

4.We are engaged in struggle,where we work and where we live,to develop self 
managed production,distribution and servicing for the world community,to meet 
human needs rather than profit.We give solidarity to others involved in these 
struggles. . _ 

5.We are fighting to abolish all authoritarian institutions,such as the state 
(including its communist variety) ,capitalism, patriarchy,and all hierarchic 
and oppressive divisions between people. 

6.We have no country and are organised on an international basis in opposition 
to oppression everywhere. S..- % 

The ASF was formed in 1986 from groups and individuals believing in the 
principles of anarcho-syndicalism.It is the Australian section of the Inter- 
national Workers’ Association.This was formed in 1922 and has national 
sections in more than a dozen countries.
