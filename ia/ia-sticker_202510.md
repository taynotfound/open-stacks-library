ee Weseabery he best place to get ideas for stickers and designing is 
by looking at other people’s art. Keep in mind the size of the sticker 
and how it will fit on an A4 page. 


I usually split the page into 8 for rectangles or 12 for squares/circles 
as shown below. 


Its good to think about how the sticker might look over time after the 
colours fade. Red is usually the fastest to fade in the sunlight so try to 
incorporate some black in a way that the sticker is still readable over 
time. 


It's best to get a used inkjet printer. They can be quite old 
and it doesn’t matter too much. Try to find one that has a glossy photo 
paper option. It might be worth it to find a CISS printer like an eco tank 
because ink cartridges are a total scam. CISS printers are also expensive 
so I'll teach you how to shoplift ink cartridges in the UK. 


Most major supermarkets like Tesco, Asda, Sainsburys, etc. will sell your 
printer’s official brand ink cartridges or their own discount version of it. 
They may seem almost impossible to shoplift because they have an RFID 
sticker on the inside of the packaging and often a large plastic safety tag 
on the outside but there's a pretty simple solution. 


You'll need to bring a shoulder bag with say a small piece of clothing to 

make it look full, a small blade/scissors that you can easily use inside the 
bag. Wearing a mask might put a target on your back so if you just wear 
a cap, cover tattoos and don't look directly at any cameras, you'll be fine. 


Take the ink cartridge pack off the shelf and when you are in an aisle 

by yourself slip it into the bag. Move somewhere else in the store and 
act like you are looking for something in your bag. Use the blade you 
brought with you to slice the plastic packaging, take out the individual 
cartridges and discard the rest of the packaging elsewhere. Make sure 
the paper RFID tag has not fallen into your bag and then leave the store. 
You may want to purchase something small so that you don't appear as 
suspicious. 


You should either do this when the store is quite busy and security are 
unable to follow you or when the store is very quiet so there is less likely 
to be a security guard watching live footage at the entrance. In the UK, 
security guards are mostly there for intimidation and company policy 
usually states that they can't leave the property to chase someone. 


If you've done this right the security gates won't go off but if they do, 
just keep walking like you didn't hear anything. Security alarms go off 
constantly every day and its usually just because an employee forgot to 
take a tag off. 


Good sticker paper is hard to find in store so you'll have to just order it 
online. Try to find splash-proof or waterproof sticker paper 


Geyser Test print your design with plain paper so that you don't 
waste any sticker paper sheets. Then switch to the ‘glossy photo paper’ 
option on your printer. After printing 1 sheet, you should throw up one 
of the stickers to test if its weather proof. If it isn’t you can try using ei- 
ther hairspray or varnish spray to protect the design. Even if the stickers 
get ruined in the rain or get torn down they’ still very useful to cover 
up fascist stickers. 


You could get a trimmer for cutting the sheets but a craft knife will do 
the job just fine. 


When you print the design, part of the paper will be left blank but that 
can be very useful. If you use a craft knife to lightly cut the boarders, you 
can take just the top layer off and keep the backing. This makes it easier 
to peel the sticker off and throw it up. 


If you're planning on distributing the stickers, you can also print your 
activist groups site/insta tag as a watermark on the backing paper so that 
people can throw up the sticker and keep the backing paper to look you 
up online once they get home. 


EMOSTY This is the fun part. If you live in a very conservative area, be 
careful but most of the time you won't have any problems. Unless there's 
cops around you'll be fine to throw up stickers. 


Stickers are the best way to distribute leftist propaganda, they’re easy and 
fun to share and use. Its best to give out quite a few stickers because you 
wont be able sticker your whole city on your own.
