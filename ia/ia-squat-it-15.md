Dear Squatters Union of Victoria,

regular bulletins to the rest of the Ecomedia network. : 


Obviously, we can't keep in touch with everything that's going on around 
Australia from Sydney, so we're writing to ask you if you're interested 
in helping us to set up an Australia-wide alternative news network. 


What we have in mind is an office in each capital city working on a 
similar basis to the Sydney office. We will handle the international 
links for the whole of Australia - to save money and keep it simple - 
and the offices in each state will act as collectors of local news which 
will be circulated around the other states as well as overseas (via 
Sydney ) They will also act as distributers of national and inter- 
national news to a network of subscribers within their state.

have!

in any way. We will send you more details when we've worked them out.

the ruling class has kept us isolated from each other for too long. This
